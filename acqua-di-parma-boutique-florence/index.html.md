# Step Inside Acqua di Parma's New Florence Boutique and...

Walking through the enchanting streets of Florence feels like stepping into a vivid painting where every corner tells a unique story. Recently, Florence unveiled a dazzling new addition to its artistic narrative - the **Acqua di Parma** boutique located at Via degli Strozzi 28R. As you step inside, it's as if you’re entering a sensory wonderland where fragrance and design harmoniously mingle. _The brand’s_ new store embodies the true essence of Italian elegance and heritage, wrapped in a modern-day allure.

The genius behind this architectural marvel is [Dorothée Meilichzon](https://www.instagram.com/dorothemelichzon), whose touch transforms the boutique into a visual vocabulary of Florence’s essence. Imagine vaulted niches, the quadrilobo motif, and stunning marble stripes weaving a graphic narrative on the walls and ceilings. These elements culminate into a space that not only shops but speaks - the language of Acqua di Parma. As a visiting 25-year-old woman venturing through Florence, you might find yourself blissfully lost in this fragrant labyrinth, eager to explore its delightful offerings. Isn’t it exciting when famous brands like [Acqua di Parma](https://www.instagram.com/acquadiparma) become part of such vibrant urban tales?

## A Visual Delight Awaits You

_
Photo: @acquadiparma

Stepping into Acqua di Parma is like entering a golden sanctuary, where the inviting interplay of warm yellows and oranges greets you from every corner. Each niche displays an exquisite range of fragrances and luxury items. The inviting layout and decor not only showcase the meticulously crafted products but also invite you to touch, feel, and let yourself be surrounded by elegance. It's a place where the aesthetics match the quality of Acqua di Parma’s_ iconic products. You might find yourself lingering over an Eau de Cologne costing $85 or a luxurious hand cream priced at $35.
