# 7 Amazing Guerlain Perfumes to Try ...

**Guerlain Perfumes** are a classic. Guerlain is one of the oldest perfume houses in the world, and is held in high regard by perfumeries. Not only do the perfumes by Guerlain smell great, but the bottles in which the perfume is housed are just as appealing. They scream of an old Hollywood style of glamour. If you’re after a Guerlain perfume, try a few of the following …

## 1. Guerlain ‘Shalimar Parfum Initial’ Perfume

![Guerlain ‘Shalimar Parfum Initial’ Perfume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/1_guerlain-lsquo-shalimar-parfum-initial-perfume_182x250.jpg)

**Price:** from $84.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This **Guerlain perfume** has an amber-floral fragrance. It has notes of bergamot, rose, vanilla and tonka bean. The overall effect is a rich, sensual fragrance. It also comes in a striking bottle with a stylish tassel detail.

## 2. Guerlain ‘My Insolence’ Perfume

![Guerlain ‘My Insolence’ Perfume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/2_guerlain-lsquo-my-insolence-perfume_248x224.jpg)

**Price:** from $56.00 at [sephora.com](https://goo.gl/09hxT3)
If feminine, floral scents are your signature then this is definitely a perfume worth checking out. It has notes of jasmine, raspberry, patchouli, and almond blossom. To top it all off, the packaging is equally as pretty as the scent, with a large rose lid adorning a sleek spherical bottle.

## 3. Guerlain ‘L’Heure Bleue’ Perfume

![Guerlain ‘L’Heure Bleue’ Perfume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/3_guerlain-lsquo-lheure-bleue-perfume_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $71.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Guerlain is a French brand, so it’s no wonder that many of the **perfumes by Guerlain** are inspired by Parisian living. The name of this perfume translates into ‘The Blue Hour’ and is inspired by the magic of a Parisian twilight. It has notes of rose, iris, and musk among others.

## 4. Guerlain ‘Aqua Allegoria Madarin-Basilic’ Perfume

![Guerlain ‘Aqua Allegoria Madarin-Basilic’ Perfume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/4_guerlain-lsquo-aqua-allegoria-madarin-basilic-perfume_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $59.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you prefer light, fresh perfumes then this one is worth trying. It has notes of white peony, blood orange, green tea, and mandarin. It’s just bursting with freshness and a scent that’s bound to put a smile on your dial.

## 5. Guerlain ‘Champs-Elysees’ Perfume

![Guerlain ‘Champs-Elysees’ Perfume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/5_guerlain-lsquo-champs-elysees-perfume_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $71.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
**Guerlain perfumes** are distinctly French and this particular perfume is named after an iconic French landmark. The Champs-Elysees was actually the birthplace of the House of Guerlain. This playful perfume contains notes of mimosa leaves, cassis berries, and almond blossom.

## 6. Guerlain ‘L’Instant De Guerlain’ Perfume

![Guerlain ‘L’Instant De Guerlain’ Perfume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/6_guerlain-lsquo-linstant-de-guerlain-perfume_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** from $73.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is a soft and sensual smelling perfume. It’s described as a blend of ‘interwoven fragrance harmonies’. It has notes of citrus honey, magnolia inclusion, and amber harmony. The overall effect is that of a sweet smelling, but not overpowering, floral perfuming.

## 7. Guerlain ‘Samsara’ Perfume

![Guerlain ‘Samsara’ Perfume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/7_guerlain-lsquo-samsara-perfume_163x250.jpg)

**Price:** $96.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This particular **Guerlain perfume** is inspired by the magic of the Orient. The perfume contains notes of bergamot, narcissus, ylang-ylang, and sandalwood. This lush smelling perfume also comes in a striking red bottle.

You can’t go past an amazing perfume, and **Guerlain perfumes** are worth trying out. What works for one person won’t always work for the next, so keep this in mind when trying out perfumes. Have you ever tried any perfumes by Guerlain and, if so, which one?
