# 11 Amazing Smelling Body Mists to Try ...

If you aren't someone that is super into perfumes, but you still want something that smells good on your skin, have you ever considered any body mists to try out? Body mists are light, they are airy and they aren't nearly as heavy as a perfume – the best part though? They are cheap! So, if you're looking for body mists to try, but haven't found the ones that work for you yet, give a look to my top 7 amazing body mists!

## 1. Sweet Temptations by Victoria's Secret

![Sweet Temptations by Victoria's Secret](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/08/1_sweet-temptations-by-victorias-secret_599x586.jpg)

Of course, the very first of my body mists to try would revolve around Victoria's Secret. This amazing body mist is all about the fruity, all about the floral and it's honestly one of those body mists that you'll want to use over and over again! It's a scent that is packed with lily of the valley, apricot, grapefruit and even some amazing floral notes! So, would you want to try this particular [body mist](https://perfumes.allwomenstalk.com/differences-between-body-mist-perfume/)?

## 2. Hawaiian Ginger by Calgon

![Hawaiian Ginger by Calgon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/08/2_hawaiian-ginger-by-calgon_599x696.jpg)

Now, I don't typically go for things that are super tropical, but I was feeling a little different when I brought this body mist. This awesome, incredible and amazing rush of exotic fruit scent will transport you right to Hawaii! It's packed with lemon, Hawaiian ginger and even some musk and woody notes! It's super light, totally airy and you'll love it!

## 3. Love Spell by Victoria's Secret

![Love Spell by Victoria's Secret](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/08/3_love-spell-by-victorias-secret_599x770.jpg)

So this scent is intoxicating, I'm going to warn you right now. It's a scent that is packed with lush cherry blossom, some juicy peach notes, some amazing floral rush notes and a finish of aloe vera and calming chamomile. It's a scent that is sexy, spicy and ever-so-beautiful as it unfolds on your skin!

## 4. Pink Grapefruit by the Body Shop

![Pink Grapefruit by the Body Shop](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/08/4_pink-grapefruit-by-the-body-shop_599x509.jpg)

All right girlies, if you are looking for a body mist that is packed with crispness, that is zingy and beautiful, this is the scent for you! It's a lighter scent that is filled with pink grapefruit extract and is ideal for someone that loves anything citrus. So, you ready to jump right into this scent?

## 5. Sweet Pea by Bath & Body Works

![Sweet Pea by Bath & Body Works](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/08/5_sweet-pea-by-bath-body-works_379x426.jpg)

While I typically think that anything sweet pea is just too sweet, this scent is ideal for anyone that wants something innocent, that wants something beautiful and that wants something a little out there. It's got some amazing violet notes and it even contains some amazing aloe vera notes! Give it a try, girlies!

## 6. Sexy Little Things by Victoria's Secret

![Sexy Little Things by Victoria's Secret](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/08/6_sexy-little-things-by-victorias-secret_427x569.jpg)

If you are looking for something that is oh-so-sexy and something that is ideal for date night (and not heavy at all!), this is the scent for you! This smell is all about the nectarine, all about the amber notes and all about the cattelaya orchid. It's a scent that really does last a little while for a body mist!

## 7. Vanilla Body Mist by the Body Shop

![Vanilla Body Mist by the Body Shop](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/08/7_vanilla-body-mist-by-the-body-shop_599x511.jpg)

If you are a girl that doesn't want to smell like flowers, but rather want something that is a little sweeter and a little less out there, this is the scent for you! It smells just like vanilla and it is the ideal scent for the office – as it lasts just long enough to finish up a day of work!

## 8. Acqua Di Gioia Hydrating Body Mist by Giorgio Armani

![lotion,skin,product,skin care,cream,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f7/de/h6hvz_lotion_skin_product_skin-care_cream_600x528.jpg)

At $45, this body mist might be a bit too pricey. However, if you're willing to make the splurge, you'll find it to be a great investment! The scent is described as a "clean, incredibly fresh, aquatic scent that is ultra-approachable, yet memorable." It's perfect for girls who think perfume can be too overwhelming.

## 9. Violet Lily Sky by Bath & Body Works

![perfume,product,lotion,cosmetics,bottle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i4/iw/xi1e4_perfume_product_lotion_cosmetics_bottle_280x348.jpg)

With this, you'll fall in love at first mist! The key fragrance notes feature "violet blooms, sparkling bergamot and pink pepper, floating on a cloud of whipped lily petals and sheer sandalwood". Sounds heavenly, right?

## 10. Dark Kiss Fine Fragrance Mist by Bath & Body Works

![perfume,violet,lotion,product,magenta,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n1/su/syr0o_perfume_violet_lotion_product_magenta_280x347.jpg)

In a seductive blend of dark berries and vanilla, you'll fall in love with this body mist! I absolutely love the scent of it - it's refreshing and still light and delightful. The conditioning aloe mist nourishes your skin!

## 11. Fresh & Clean Body Mist by Victoria's Secret Pink

![pink,product,magenta,deodorant,lotion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dj/xc/mizz6_pink_product_magenta_deodorant_lotion_404x520.jpg)

Victoria's Secret always has the best scents, right?! PINK's "Fresh & Clean" body mist smells amazing and is a must-have. The key notes are apple and lily!

So girls, these are just a couple of my top body mists that you should try! Have you ever used a body mist? Which ones? Give 'em up in the comments!
