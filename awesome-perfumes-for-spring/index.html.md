# 8 Awesome Perfumes for Spring ...

**Perfumes for spring** should ideally be light and airy, in perfect complement to the warmer weather. There are lots of fantastic spring perfumes out right now, scents that burst with floral and fruity smells. They're delicious, feminine, and subtle, plus many of them go right from day to evening, so you can enjoy some extra versatility. Check out these awesome spring fragrances, and see what you think!

## 1. Flowerbomb by Viktor & Rolf

![Flowerbomb by Viktor & Rolf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/1_flowerbomb-by-viktor-rolf_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $50.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Flowerbomb is one of the best **perfumes for spring**, hands down. Even the name of the scent evokes springtime, and the fragrance itself is to die for. There's jasmine, rose, orchid, and freesia, so you'll smell like a blooming spring garden. The base is patchouli, though, which keeps the flowers themselves from being too overpowering.

## 2. Jimmy Choo by Jimmy Choo Parfums

![Jimmy Choo by Jimmy Choo Parfums](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/2_jimmy-choo-by-jimmy-choo-parfums_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love Jimmy Choo as a **spring perfume**. It's gorgeous right down to the bottle. The scent notes are incredibly original – with a mix of green notes, orchids, and patchouli, it's perfect. However, the real kicker is the fact that it carries hints of toffee. Toffee! You won't know whether to wear it or taste it!

## 3. Billionaire Boyfriend by Boyfriend

![Billionaire Boyfriend by Boyfriend](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/3_billionaire-boyfriend-by-boyfriend_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $45.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you're into **perfumes for spring** with a rich, luxe scent, then Billionaire Boyfriend is ideal. It's amazingly sensual, making it a great nighttime fragrance. You'll love the notes of tangerine, green leaves, jasmine, orchids, gardenias, and vetiver. It also has both patchouli and sandalwood, which gives it a marvelous, androgynous musk.

## 4. L'Eau De Chloe by Chloe

![L'Eau De Chloe by Chloe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/4_leau-de-chloe-by-chloe_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $65.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Chloe always has fantastic **spring perfumes**, and this one's no exception. It's fresh, free, and fabulously feminine, so if you want something very light and delicate that isn't too overpowering, this is an excellent choice. It's very fruity, with scent notes of grapefruit and peach, along with base notes of cedar, patchouli, and amber. Perfect!

## 5. L'Essence by Balenciaga Paris

![L'Essence by Balenciaga Paris](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/5_lessence-by-balenciaga-paris_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $95.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
And you thought Balenciaga was just for bags and shoes! Not so, L'Essence is the perfect **spring perfume**. Ideal for evening wear, it's sensual, intimate, and confident. The notes are simple – violet, vetiver, and woody scents – but altogether they create a wonderfully strong, almost masculine fragrance.

## 6. Love Sweet Love by Philosophy

![Love Sweet Love by Philosophy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/6_love-sweet-love-by-philosophy_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $42.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is a wonderful **perfume for spring** as well – just the name makes me smile. It's light, charming, and wonderfully girly, with scents combining fruits and flowers. I could almost see this as the next Baby Soft, except it doesn't have that prevailing scent of baby flower, which I personally enjoyed way too much in my teens.

## 7. Summer Edition by COACH

![Summer Edition by COACH](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/7_summer-edition-by-coach_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $65.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
As you can see, this isn't just one of the best **perfumes for spring**, it's also perfect all the way into summer. It's sparkling and light but sensual and evocative as well. Light notes like lilies, magnolias, jasmine, and honeysuckle blend harmoniously with stronger scents like musk, amber, and sandalwood. The combination is absolutely stunning!

## 8. Princess Night by Vera Wang

![Princess Night by Vera Wang](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/02/8_princess-night-by-vera-wang_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Here's another **spring perfume** perfect to wear during evenings out. Part of the Princess collection, this one is darker and more decadent. It's very fruity, it scents of vanilla, wild berries, raspberries, watermelon, and orange flowers. However, thanks to the addition of roses, plumeria, musk, sugar, and woody notes, it's also quite bold.

As you can see – and smell! – there are lots of delicious, feminine **perfumes for spring** you can try. I love that patchouli, cedar, and sandalwood are becoming more popular, myself. I love woody base notes in both perfumes and colognes. Which spring fragrances are you most excited to try?
