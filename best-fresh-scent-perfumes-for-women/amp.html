Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a perfume smell 'fresh'?
Fresh-smelling perfumes often have notes like citrus, green, aquatic, or light floral scents. They remind you of a cool breeze, a freshly cut lawn, or a zesty lemon. It's all about capturing that clean, just-out-of-the-shower vibe.
Can I wear fresh scent perfumes all year round?
Totally! Fresh scents are super versatile. They're perfect for summer but also great for adding a bit of brightness during the colder months. It's like wearing a little bit of sunshine on a dreary day.
How long do fresh scent perfumes usually last?
Fresh scents tend to be lighter, so they might not last as long as heavier, more intense perfumes. But, don't worry! You can always stash a travel size in your purse for a quick refresh.
Are fresh scent perfumes good for work?
Absolutely! Fresh perfumes are generally subtle and inoffensive, making them perfect for a professional setting. They're not too overpowering, so you won't be that person in the office with the overwhelming scent.
Do fresh scent perfumes suit all ages?
Yes, they really do. Fresh fragrances have a universal appeal. They can be youthful and energetic for a younger crowd, yet sophisticated and elegant for more mature wearers. It's all about how it makes you feel!
