# 7 Calming Perfume Scents That'll Make You Smell Irresistible ...

There are calming perfume scents that can seduce, while making you feel right at home. You don't want to be stuck wearing a scent that is too strong and irritates those around you. It may take a while, but you should search for calming perfume scents that make you feel tranquil and confident. It's hard to find the perfect balance, but it's certainly possible. Here are a few ingredients you should look for while on your hunt:

## 1. Lavender

![Lavender](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rc/aw/aqmcompd_600x800.jpg)

Lavender is a sweet floral scent that is therapeutic. The word comes from the Latin name lavandus, which means ‘to wash,' which is why the smell is associated with cleanliness. It’s also contains therapeutic properties, which can relieve pain. It’s one of the calming perfume scents that smell delicious, so any man would be lucky to stand next to you.

## 2. Ylang Ylang

![Ylang Ylang](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z7/bx/t4e0kin3_600x600.jpg)

Ylang ylang is a type of flower that can help relieve many health problems. When it first grows, it has a faint scent, but after a few weeks, it grows stronger and stronger. When it is fully grown, it smells rich and resembles lavender, and is even spread on the beds of newlyweds in Indonesia. If you want to get compliments, find a perfume that contains ylang ylang, which is also known as cananga odorata.

## 3. Vetiver

![Vetiver](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f8/q1/awqbf6ay_480x640.jpg)

Vetiver is an ingredient that will make a perfume smell earthy. Recently, the demand for vetiver oil is increasing, because it is frequently used to add fragrance and flavor to different items. It cannot be recreated artificially, so if you own something with vetiver, you know that it’s the real deal.

## 4. Palmarosa

![Palmarosa](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f6/nd/zq6jpkqt_600x900.jpg)

Palmarosa oil smells similar to roses, so you know that it’s going to be irresistible. In addition to being used in perfumes, it is also used in soap and different cosmetics. If you love flowery scents, then search for something that contains palmarosa. It’s a delicious scent that won’t disappoint.

## 5. Sandalwood

![Sandalwood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/am/0w/rrd406ud_600x600.jpg)

This fragrance has a distinctive scent of wood. The demand for it is also high, but is unable to be met, because it is a protected species. If you’re able to get your hands on a perfume that contains genuine sandalwood, you should be thankful. Since it’s so rare, no one around will smell quite as good as you do.

## 6. Amber

![Amber](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gc/d8/pul0gvp2_600x569.jpg)

Amber is a rich, warm scent that is pleasing to the nose. If you've ever smelled it before, you'll remember it. It is very distinctive, and not many other scents can compete with its boldness. Head to the mall, and you're bound to find shelves full of amber scented products.

## 7. Patchouli Oil

![Patchouli Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z0/0k/hn1ylipu_600x600.jpg)

This is another earthy scent, but it is also very nutty-sweet, and some say it smells a bit like dirt. Now, that doesn't sound like your ideal perfume, but mixed with other ingredients, it can be fantastic. You'll never know until you test it, so get to the store and sample some.

The perfect perfume can make you feel confident, relaxed, beautiful, or all of the above. You just have to know what to look for instead of blindly smelling every bottle you come across. Try to find a perfume that contains one or more of the scents listed above, and you can't go wrong! What's the name of your favorite perfume?
