## 1. Origins of Carrière Frères

![](https://img.allw.mn/thumbs/69/phzm3zczoyi45f80zn04rn_993x1308.jpg)

Imagine stepping into the heart of 19th century Paris. Among the cobblestone streets and the silhouette of the Eiffel Tower emerging in the cityscape, a story of luxurious scents begins. **The Carrière brothers**, visionary entrepreneurs, seized the moment in 1884 to establish Carrière Frères.  They didn't just join the league of Parisian wax makers; they sought to redefine it, mixing their **passions for botany** and purity with the traditional art. **Wax making** in Paris was the realm of craftsmen - a vocation where skill whispered loudly of one's heritage and dedication. But these brothers? They were on to something extraordinary, combining their lineage within the trade to a burgeoning curiosity about the natural world. Their workshop was more than a space; it was the cradle of an olfactory revolution.
