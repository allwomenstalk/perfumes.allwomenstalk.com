# Carriere Freres Winter Collection \- 3 Divine Scented Candles that Celebrate The New Season

**Carriere Freres Winter Collection - 3 Divine Scented Candles that Celebrate The New Season …**

**There is a particular poetry to winter** - a serene hush that descends upon the world as the first frost touches the earth, the way light refracts off freshly fallen snow, and the comforting ritual of gathering near a warm fire. For those who savor the subtleties of the season, the **French candle atelier Carrière Frères** has created a collection that transforms the intangible beauty of winter into fragrance. [Their Winter Collection, composed of Winter Fire, Winter Flower, and Winter Forest](https://carrierefreres.com/us_en/gifts/winter-collection.html), is a **trilogy of scents** that celebrates the spirit of the cold months in all their nuanced elegance.

For nearly a century, **Carrière Frères has been synonymous with craftsmanship, quality, and the art of scent.** Founded in 1910, the brand has become an icon in the world of fine home fragrances, renowned for its luxurious candles and diffusers that transform interiors into evocative, aromatic sanctuaries. **The Winter Collection continues this tradition**, offering three unique compositions that each embody a **distinct facet of the season.**

**1.Winter Forest - The Whispering Woodlands**

**

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/google-doc-ref-image1-1762298902459.png)

**

**Price: USD $85.00**

**Key Notes: Pine, Eucalyptus, Moss**

There is a certain stillness that descends upon a snow-dusted forest, a hush that invites reflection and reverie. [Carrière Frères’ Winter Forest](https://carrierefreres.com/us_en/gifts/winter-collection/winter-forest-candle.html) candle captures this sensation with a precise, olfactory fidelity that transports the senses to the heart of towering evergreens.

Opening with crisp top notes of fir, juniper, and eucalyptus, Winter Forest immediately evokes the sensation of a brisk woodland walk, where every breath carries the earthy aroma of winter flora. A heart of cedarwood deepens the experience, while moss lends a subtle dampness reminiscent of forest floors hidden beneath a delicate layer of frost. The effect is both grounding and invigorating - a sensory embrace that reconnects one with the quiet majesty of nature.

Crafted with 100% vegetable wax from European rapeseed, the candle burns evenly for 40 to 45 hours, making it a lasting companion through the longest evenings. The vegan formula, made in France, reflects Carrière Frères’ commitment to sustainability without compromising on luxury. In essence, Winter Forest is more than a candle - it is a forest path wandered at leisure, a meditation on the serenity of winter itself.

**2. Winter Flower - A Floral Interlude for Cold Afternoons**

**

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/google-doc-ref-image2-1762298903583.png)

**

**Price: USD $85.00**

**Key Notes: Spices, White Flowers, Sandalwood**

While winter is often associated with stark landscapes and crisp air, there is another dimension to the season - a warmth found in intimate moments indoors, where light fades slowly and the world outside becomes a muted canvas of white. [Winter Flower](https://carrierefreres.com/us_en/gifts/winter-collection/winter-flower-candle.html) embodies this quiet elegance, offering a fragrance that is both indulgent and comforting.

The candle opens with the gentle spice of tea leaves, conjuring the aromatic ritual of a steaming cup prepared to warm the soul. Its heart blooms with winter rose, gardenia, and an ensemble of white flowers, creating a luminous bouquet that feels at once sophisticated and tender. The fragrance then settles into a base of sandalwood and patchouli, providing depth, stability, and the quiet satisfaction of a winter afternoon cocooned from the elements.

Designed with the same 100% vegetable rapeseed wax, Winter Flower offers 40 to 45 hours of burn time. Its vegan composition ensures that indulgence and conscientious craftsmanship coexist harmoniously. In its essence, Winter Flower is a fragrant embrace - a floral interlude that softens the chill and invites contemplation, evoking the simple luxury of being fully present in a moment of warmth and beauty.

**3. Winter Fire - The Hearth’s Warmth Captured**

**

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/google-doc-ref-image3-1762298906392.png)

**

**Price: USD $85.00**

**Key Notes: Cedar, Pine, Incense**

Few experiences encapsulate winter more vividly than the comforting allure of a fireplace - the gentle crackle of wood, the amber glow reflecting off walls, and the subtle smoke that hints at warmth and familiarity. Winter Fire distills this beloved ritual into a refined, aromatic journey.

The composition begins with robust notes of cedarwood and balsam fir, layered over a heart of pine and cashmere wood. Anchored by a rich base of amber and incense, [Winter Fire](https://carrierefreres.com/us_en/gifts/winter-collection/winter-fire-candle.html) conjures the vivid imagery of a log catching flame, a moment suspended in time where the fire’s warmth seems to permeate not only the room but the spirit itself. It is a fragrance that invites stillness, yet resonates with vitality - the kind of scent that can transform a living space into a sanctuary during the season’s darkest evenings.

Like its Winter Collection counterparts, Winter Fire is made with 100% European rapeseed wax, **offering 40 to 45 hours of clean, even burn**. Its vegan and French-made formulation underscores Carrière Frères’ commitment to excellence, sustainability, and sensory sophistication. With Winter Fire, the familiar comfort of the hearth becomes not just a visual or tactile experience, but an aromatic one, elevating winter’s simple pleasures into an art form.
