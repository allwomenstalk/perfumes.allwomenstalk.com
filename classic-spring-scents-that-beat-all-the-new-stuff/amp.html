21 Classic Scents That Beat All the New Stuff ...

Viktor &amp; Rolf Flowerbomb • MIU MIU • Marc Jacobs Daisy • CHANEL N°5 Eau De Parfum • DKNY Be Delicious • More ...

By Jennifer

Classic scents are so called for a reason. They are enduring and women love them. Rather than trying and tossing dozens of new perfumes, why not rely on one or two tried-and-true scents? There are so many classic scents to choose from, and you already know you love them... let's hear it for your new (old) perfume!

1. Viktor & Rolf Flowerbomb

Price: $85 at sephora.com
There's nothing more perfect than an explosion of floral scent, and this perfume is the classic flowerbomb.

2. MIU MIU

Price: $88 at sephora.com
I adore Lily of the Valley, and this classic scent starts with those sweet tones and finishes with more fab floral.

3. Marc Jacobs Daisy

Price: $78 at sephora.com
This is one of my all-time favorite perfumes! It's warm and sweet and innocent, ideal for use any day of the year.

4. CHANEL N°5 Eau De Parfum

Price: $100 at sephora.com
There's a reason this classic is still so popular - it's just that good, that versatile, that luxe.

5. DKNY Be Delicious

Price: $68 at sephora.com
This fun, flirty, fruity scent is my new (old) scent. The green apples just draw me in every time!

6. Clinique Happy

Price: $50 at sephora.com
Sunshine, warm temps, open-toe shoes, and flowers: those things make me happy and that's what this perfume is all about

7. Thierry Mugler Angel

Price: $80 at sephora.com
Revel in your softer side with this classic scent. It's so delicate, so soft!

8. Fresh Sugar Lemon

Price: $50 at sephora.com
Lemon: bright, vivid, fun! That sums up the scent of your days in the best way!

9. Ralph Lauren Ralph

Price: $60 at sephora.com
It seems like all of the Ralph Lauren scents are instant classics, and this one's no exception. You'll love it!

10. Marc Jacobs Honey

Price: $78 at sephora.com
If you love the floral tones in Daisy, but you're more of a fruity-tones girl, this scent was made just for you. It's all warm fruits, like pear and mandarin.

11. Dior Pure Poison

Price: $94 at sephora.com
This is all about your new fling, where floral tones meet warm amber. So seductive!

12. My Burberry

Price: $95 at sephora.com
There's something very British about rose tones, and this perfume blends rosees with geranium leaf for another classic scent.

13. Estée Lauder Beautiful

Price: $74 at sephora.com
Floral, citrus and woods - a lush blend of these three tones creates a warm scent that will carry you through all of your days.

14. Stella McCartney POP

Price: $72 at sephora.com
This smells fab to me, with fresh florals, warm earth, and a little fruitiness thrown in for good measure, too.

15. Jour D'Hermès

Price: $113 at sephora.com
This is the signature feminine scent from the powerhouse brand, all fresh, lovely floral tones.

16. Hanae Mori Butterfly Eau De Parfum

Price: $75 at sephora.com
Celebrate the 20-year anniversary of this perfume by enjoying its sweet strawberry-toned scent.

17. Tory Burch

Price: $90 at sephora.com
This is the brand's first scent, but it's already a classic, with its floral tones but versatile feel.

18. Bobbi Brown Beach Fragrance

Price: $74 at sephora.com
Warm sunshine, soft sea breeze, sweet jasmine, and bright mandarin - what could be more perfect?

19. Michael Kors Coral

Price: $75 at amazon.com
This limited-edition scent is already a cult classic, so if you see it in stock, snap it up right away.

20. Guerlain Shalimar Eau De Parfum

Price: $104 at sephora.com
With warm sunshine tones of vanilla, bergamot, and amber, this classic scent is just so romantic!

21. Calvin Klein Ck One

Price: $49 at sephora.com
I know what you're thinking: this scent is still around? It is, and for good reason. It's as pure, fresh, and clean-smelling as ever. Why not give it another try?
