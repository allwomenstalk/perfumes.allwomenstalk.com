# 21 Classic Scents That Beat All the New Stuff ...

**Classic scents** are so called for a reason. They are enduring and women love them. Rather than trying and tossing dozens of new perfumes, why not rely on one or two tried-and-true scents? There are so many **classic scents** to choose from, and you already know you love them... let's hear it for your new (old) perfume!

## 1. Viktor & Rolf Flowerbomb

![Viktor & Rolf Flowerbomb](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r3/wi/q76k2zv756eec3f4bacf1771965620_600x556.jpg)

Price: $85 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
There's nothing more perfect than an explosion of floral scent, and this perfume is the classic flowerbomb.

## 2. MIU MIU

![MIU MIU](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bc/qe/yv0gwh2r56eec3f71f12e246462019_600x582.jpg)

Price: $88 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I adore Lily of the Valley, and this classic scent starts with those sweet tones and finishes with more fab floral.

## 3. Marc Jacobs Daisy

![Marc Jacobs Daisy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lp/mh/fn48ngln56eec3f691265472471454_600x581.jpg)

Price: $78 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is one of my all-time favorite perfumes! It's warm and sweet and innocent, ideal for use any day of the year.

## 4. CHANEL N°5 Eau De Parfum

![CHANEL N°5 Eau De Parfum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gg/k6/h91ixwd756eec3f7116c2347268181_600x499.jpg)

Price: $100 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
There's a reason this classic is still so popular - it's just that good, that versatile, that luxe.

## 5. DKNY Be Delicious

![DKNY Be Delicious](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rh/ss/pntlcckh56eec3f7ccd5a642128264_600x478.jpg)

## 6. Clinique Happy

![Clinique Happy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e5/ly/il0015gw56eec3f7279f9265638778_600x588.jpg)

## 7. Thierry Mugler Angel

![Thierry Mugler Angel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vx/wi/djknth0356eec3f7a4a07411521541_600x580.jpg)

## 8. Fresh Sugar Lemon

![Fresh Sugar Lemon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w0/by/lx34znlo56eec3f86d7f4252859148_600x589.jpg)

## 9. Ralph Lauren Ralph

![Ralph Lauren Ralph](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ob/v0/j7g7iiyx56eec3f9487f0237096548_600x539.jpg)
