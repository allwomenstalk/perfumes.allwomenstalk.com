11 Deliciously Sweet Perfumes That Smell like Candy ...

By Heather • 47 Comments

Deliciously sweet perfumes have a unique way of taking us back to the carefree days of our youth, don’t they? I mean, who hasn't chased their siblings around the house with a mouth-watering lollipop in hand? As we step into 2024, the world of perfumes seems even more delicious with a bounty of scents that mirror our favorite childhood candies. From tempting caramel to irresistible chocolate, these fragrances are here to satisfy your sweet tooth without the calorie count.

Speaking of fragrances, it’s like stepping into Willy Wonka’s Factory – only instead of chocolate fountains and everlasting gobstoppers, it's rows upon rows of delectable perfume bottles that beg to be sniffed. You see, I'm a bit of a perfume hoarder. Not in the obsessive 'I-need-every-fragrance-ever-made' kind of way, but more in the 'I-cannot-resist-anything-that-reminds-me-of-dessert' sense. Trust me, there’s nothing like spritzing on a bit of sugary goodness to lift your spirits and turn heads.

When it comes to finding the perfect perfume that smells like candy, it’s not as easy as strolling into a candy shop and pointing to whatever looks the yummiest. Oh no, my friend, it requires a bit of finesse, a touch of gourmand expertise, and perhaps a generous sprinkling of curiosity. For those who can't get enough of gourmand perfumes, wait until you dive into the world of vanilla-scented delights.

This year, I've dedicated a significant portion of my time (and nostrils) to conducting this oh-so-sweet research. I've sniffed, sprayed, and lingered in many a perfume aisle, all for the noble cause of bringing you this comprehensive guide. And I must say, the journey was anything but bitter. Besides, who wouldn’t want to be known as the friend who always smells like a freshly baked cookie?

The fun doesn’t stop at smelling like candy. These delicious scents also come with their own set of notes and undertones. Think about blending sugary confections with a hint of floral or fruity zest. Scents that smell like candy aren’t just sweet; they’re dynamic.

I must say, cats nearly followed me home a few times when wearing some of these scents – a true testament to their sweetness! Now, whether you’re a chocolate lover or someone who melts at the sight of marshmallows, this guide is about to become your best read of the year. So, sit back, relax and prepare to be enveloped in a vibrant, sensory experience where every spritz is a delightful trip down memory lane.

Flowerbomb by Viktor & Rolf is a delightful sweet perfume that will please any candy lover.
