# DIY Wild Raspberry Rose Infusion Perfume ...

Who said water and vodka were only for drinking? Learn how to utilize both in this video on **how to make wild raspberry rose infusion perfume**!

Obviously, you need **raspberries** and **rose petals**. Let them soak in the sun while in small jars filled with 1/2 water and 1/2 vodka.

Of course, it would be easier to just use essential oil. But what woman would not want to smell like fresh raspberries and fresh rose petals? Let a man get a whiff of it and whew! You are going to turn heads! (and get a few compliments too!)

Do you know someone that likes to pick raspberries? Maybe pay them to pick some for you. While you are at it, buy yourself some roses.

Personally, I would use spray bottles that you can buy at dollar stores or on-line.

You deserve it!
