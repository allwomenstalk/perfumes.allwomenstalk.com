# 7 Essential Oils That You Can Use as Perfume ...

Essential oils are extremely fragrant, which is why there are many essential oils to use as perfume. While some essential oils have a very pungent scent, there are essential oils that have lovely fragrances that are perfect for perfume. This is really good news, because many perfumes contain harmful synthetic ingredients, like phthalates, that can interfere with your hormones. Essential oils, on the other hand, are not synthetic and can actually have healing properties. Wouldn’t you rather look for essential oils to use as perfume, than use a harmful synthetic fragrance? All of the following essential oils make wonderful perfumes, and they all have health benefits as well!

## 1. Jasmine Essential Oil

![Jasmine Essential Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tg/jz/jjpkxf98_500x623.jpg)

Jasmine essential oil is one of many essential oils to use as perfume. It is expensive, but for a special occasion it is worth the cost. This oil has a sweet, floral scent that will make you feel like you are walking through a jasmine garden. In addition to its intoxicating scent, jasmine oil also has the ability to decrease anxiety. So, you may want to consider wearing it for your wedding day! You will smell wonderful and your wedding jitters will be released.

## 2. Rose Essential Oil

![Rose Essential Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xj/ic/h3jvd1bv_600x896.jpg)

Rose essential oil has a wonderful rose scent. It is just like sticking your nose in a bouquet of roses! That being said, it can be a bit strong, which is why you should use this oil judiciously. One drop will have you smelling lovely.

## 3. Patchouli Essential Oil

![Patchouli Essential Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tb/oh/jk137uxs_600x775.jpg)

Patchouli is most often associated with the hippies of the 1960s, but it is still a scent that many people love. It has a rich, earthy fragrance that works for both men and women, and it can be calming and grounding.

## 4. Ylang Ylang Essential Oil

![Ylang Ylang Essential Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u5/fi/rt4wby4f_600x812.jpg)

Ylang ylang essential oil is made with ylang ylang flowers, and it has a calming floral fragrance that is delightful and can help you feel harmonious and balanced. It is also a romantic fragrance. In Indonesia, ylang ylang flowers are spread on top of the beds of newlyweds! Just a little tidbit to keep in mind.

## 5. Vetiver Essential Oil

![Vetiver Essential Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jc/th/jtad77of_600x600.jpg)

If you like scents that are woodsey, vetiver is the scent for you. Vetiver essential oil is made from the roots of vetiver plants. Needless to say, it has an earthy scent that is grounding. The scent has also been said to help with insomnia, anxiety, and ADHD.

## 6. Orange Essential Oil

![Orange Essential Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/an/u3/icm4jnrk_600x759.jpg)

Orange essential oil is perfect for those of you who like citrus scents. It has a wonderful, uplifting aroma that will keep your mood up whenever you wear it. This is one essential oil that you don’t want to put directly on your skin because it can be irritating by itself. Rather, you should mix it with a neutral oil, like jojoba oil, before using it.

## 7. Vanilla Essential Oil

![Vanilla Essential Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jl/hn/kzlb1osi_459x574.jpg)

Vanilla is a popular scent in many perfumes, and in some body sprays it is the only scent. If you like the sweet smell of vanilla fragrances, vanilla essential oil will give you that same cupcake scent without any synthetic ingredients.

Essential oils are often used for aromatherapy. However, they also make wonderful replacements for perfume. They smell wonderful and they can even improve your mood. Which of these essential oils would you use for a perfume?
