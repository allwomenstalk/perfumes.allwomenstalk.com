# 11 Fall Body Mists You've Got to Try ...

Now that fall is here, you don't really want to keep smelling like coconuts and summer – so why not slip into some fall body mists that smell amazing and will keep the fall spirit going? I've got the top 11 fall body mists that are not only perfect for the hoodie nights, but they are light enough that you can spritz them on multiple times throughout the night to really amp up the smell!

## 1. Sweet Cinnamon Pumpkin by Bath & Body Works

![Sweet Cinnamon Pumpkin by Bath & Body Works](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/22010440_7578_434x539.jpg)

By far, one of the best fall body mists I've ever smelled is this one. It's packed with pumpkin scent without being overpowering and the layering of pumpkin to crisp apple and spiced vanilla, along with cinnamon, is absolutely amazing! This is by far one of the best body mists that I've ever spritzed onto my skin and I know that you'll love it too!

## 2. Aspen Caramel Woods by Bath & Body Works

![Aspen Caramel Woods by Bath & Body Works](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/22010443_3441_599x742.jpg)

If pumpkin isn't your thing, why not slip into something that smells just like the woods? It's a scent that is packed with juicy plum, whipped caramel, some amazing bourbon vanilla and some incredible jasmine blossom too! It's truly a great fall [body mist](https://perfumes.allwomenstalk.com/differences-between-body-mist-perfume/) that I know you'll love!

## 3. Marshmallow by Calgon

![Marshmallow by Calgon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/22010441_7813_291x500.jpg)

One of my favorite hobbies in the fall is all about sitting in front of a camp fire and roasting marshmallows and making s'mores. What better way to smell like a marshmallow and to really embrace fall than to slip into a marshmallow scented body mist? This is packed with a sweet scent, along with hints of brown sugar, vanilla and sandalwood! It's amazing!

## 4. Twilight Woods by Bath & Body Works

![Twilight Woods by Bath & Body Works](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/22010440_9072_434x552.jpg)

If woods are your thing but you want woods that smell a little sweeter and a little more enchanted, this is the scent for you! It's packed with Tuscan cypress, mimosa petals and even some amazing apricot nectar hints. It's a beautiful scent that lasts a long time for a body mist!

## 5. Feathered Musk by Victoria's Secret

![Feathered Musk by Victoria's Secret](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/22010439_2277_423x538.jpg)

If musk is something that you are super into but you don't want something that is really heavy, this is the scent for you! One note, it's a limited edition, so it might only be around for fall! It's packed with cherry noir, whipped truffle and even some amazing musk scents (hence the name!). It's a warm scent that you'll fall for instantly!

## 6. Toasted Vanilla Sugar by Bodycology

![Toasted Vanilla Sugar by Bodycology](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/22010439_9087_409x390.jpg)

In the fall, I love things that are cozy and this particular scent is all about being cozy and comforting. It's a body mist that is packed with warm vanilla, amazing amber and some beautiful brown sugar notes! It's a scent that is perfect for cozying up in the fall!

## 7. Sheer Amber by Victoria's Secret

![Sheer Amber by Victoria's Secret](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/22010440_3224_423x538.jpg)

This is another one of my favorite fall body mists – the reason? It's because it is so warm! It's packed with apple blossom, velvet amber and even some amazing lotus flower! It's a longer-lasting scent that will stay on your skin for hours and hours! I love it!

## 8. Enchanted Forest by Bodycology

![Enchanted Forest by Bodycology](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/22010440_4169_409x385.jpg)

Now, this scent is not only packed with some woody notes, it's actually got some melon and cucumber notes too! It's truly an amazing combination and it smells so clean, crisp and fresh! It also lasts a really long time, which means that you won't have to keep reapplying it!

## 9. Brown Sugar & Fig by Bath & Body Works

![Brown Sugar & Fig by Bath & Body Works](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/22010444_7633_599x742.jpg)

If fig is your favorite fall flavor and fragrance, this is the scent for you! This is a scent that is all about the coconut milk, brown sugar and fig! It's an online exclusive fragrance, so keep that in mind whenever you are buying it, but it's so, so worth it! Personally, I love this scent so much!

## 10. Play It Spicy by Playboy

![Play It Spicy by Playboy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/22010441_5489_599x575.jpg)

Yes, I know, this is a body mist by Playboy, but it's one that is oh-so-amazing too! It's got some citrus notes in it, some amazing mellow tones and it's truly one of the best body mists for fall that I've ever smelled! It's packed with spice, some floral notes and it lasts a while too!

## 11. Vermont Honey Apple by Bath & Body Works

![Vermont Honey Apple by Bath & Body Works](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/22010444_1814_599x742.jpg)

Finally, another scent that is absolutely beautiful in the fall is all about the honey apples! This amazing body mist is full of golden honey, creamy peony and even some pear and of course, crisp apple! It's sweet, it's crisp and it's ever-so-beautiful!

So, now that you know what fall body mists you should take a look at buying, what are some of your favorites? Do you have any fall scents that you look for over and over again when fall rolls in?
