# 8 Floral Scented Perfumes to Try ...

With all of the different spring scents out there, I thought that figuring out the best floral perfumes of all time would help sort out some spring fragrances for you! If you've been dying to find the right floral perfumes for you, take a look at my list below! Not only do these scents smell awesome, but they are super flowery!

## 1. Pretty Hot by Elizabeth Arden

![Pretty Hot by Elizabeth Arden](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/04/1_pretty-hot-by-elizabeth-arden_300x240.jpg)

This is one of my favorite floral perfumes out there. Not only is it wonderfully sexy and ideal for date night, but it's got some notes that are super complex in it too! Blood orange, red apple, red currant and fresh mandarin are all in there! Where are the flowers? Well, petalia, pimento blossom, peony and georgywood are all notes in this amazing perfume!

## 2. Reveal by Halle Berry

![Reveal by Halle Berry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/04/2_reveal-by-halle-berry_162x268.jpg)

Now, I typically don't love celebrity fragrances. That's just me, but I love this one. Not only does it have some incredible notes of peaches, red berries, mimosa and honeydew melon, but it's also got some iris blossom, plumeria flowers and neroli petals. It's an amazing scent that unfolds -- like a flower!

## 3. Daisy by Marc Jacobs

![Daisy by Marc Jacobs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/04/3_daisy-by-marc-jacobs_250x250.jpg)

I don't think that any floral perfume list would be complete without this amazing scent! Not only does it unfold with jasmine petals, vanilla, white woods and violet leaves, but it's got some strawberry notes and ruby red grapefruit to it too! It's light, airy and so, so pretty!

## 4. Romance by Ralph Lauren

![Romance by Ralph Lauren](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/04/4_romance-by-ralph-lauren_250x250.jpg)

This is one of the most timeless floral scents out there! Not only has this particular scent been around for quite a while, but it's for all seasons! Sungoddess rose, marigold, ginger and yellow freesia along with a splash of white violet and lotus flower. This is definitely one of the best floral scents if you really want to smell like a flower!

## 5. Poppy Flower by Coach

![Poppy Flower by Coach](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/04/5_poppy-flower-by-coach_250x250.jpg)

If you are looking for something that is super whimsical and so much fun, this is the scent for you! Not only is it packed with flowery notes, but it's full of some really deep emotions too! Jasmine petals, water lily, rose, amber and violet leaves are just a few notes that you'll find in this amazing perfume!

## 6. Flight of Fancy by Anna Sui

![Flight of Fancy by Anna Sui](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/04/6_flight-of-fancy-by-anna-sui_250x250.jpg)

I love everything that Anna Sui puts together in the way of perfume. Not only are her scents completely fresh and clean, but this one in particular is so floral, it's amazing! It's got some lychee notes, rose blossom, star magnolia, purple rain freesia and some beautiful java lemon notes to it! If you want floral, this is the one for you!

## 7. Omnia Amethyste by Bvlgari

![Omnia Amethyste by Bvlgari](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/04/7_omnia-amethyste-by-bvlgari_168x224.jpg)

I actually love the bottle on this one. I think that it is so, so unique, so different and that it's an amazing scent overall. It's got Bulgarian rosebud, heliotrope, solar wood and green sap notes all mixed in with iris flower and pink grapefruit. It's a scent that'll stay with you and that is so elegant!

## 8. Rose the One by Dolce & Gabbana

![Rose the One by Dolce & Gabbana](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/04/8_rose-the-one-by-dolce-gabbana_250x250.jpg)

Finally, if you want the ultimate floral scent, you've got to get a whiff of Rose The One by Dolce & Gabbana! It's got some amber, musk, plum and Madonna lily notes to it, but it's also packed with peach, muguet, Bulgarian rose and peony!

So, these are some of my favorite floral perfumes! Do you ladies have any other floral perfumes that you love? Give it all up here!
