# 8 Amazing Fresh Summer Scents You Need to Try ...

Summer is right around the corner, and with summer comes a brand new set of summer scents to try! Summer scents should be scents that are not too heavy, that are airy and that won't weigh you down. In the winter, you are indoors, so you want something that will last for a really long time, but in the summer, you want something lighter, something that will soak in the sunlight and ocean air that you'll be experiencing! For my top summer perfumes, give a look below!

## 1. Romance Summer Blossom by Ralph Lauren

![Romance Summer Blossom by Ralph Lauren](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/1_romance-summer-blossom-by-ralph-lauren_250x250.jpg)

No summer scents list would be complete without this unforgettable and amazingly delicious scent! It's perfect for a night out on the town or for the latest BBQ at your friend's house. It's light enough to wear everyday and it's perfect for the summer! It's full of mandarin, guava, jasmine and a bit of coconut water! You'll never, ever want to give this scent up once you try it! It's full of sunshine and butterflies – along with a splash of romance!

## 2. Nectarine Blossom & Honey by Jo Malone

![Nectarine Blossom & Honey by Jo Malone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/2_nectarine-blossom-honey-by-jo-malone_350x537.jpg)

If you are a girl that loves the scent of fruit … but you don't exactly want to smell like you are a teenager or that you are trying to be too young, this is the scent for you! It's inspired by some delicious nectarines and the taste of sweet peach sorbet. It's got some hints of plum, black currant and finally some honey. It's a seductive and beautiful scent that is ideal for summer!

## 3. Lovestruck Floral Rush by Vera Wang

![Lovestruck Floral Rush by Vera Wang](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/3_lovestruck-floral-rush-by-vera-wang_600x529.jpg)

I am a huge fan of everything that Ms. Wang does, but this particular scent? It's amazing! It's got some floral notes, some juicy fruit notes and even some amazing pink pepper notes! It smells just like summer to me, with the apricot blossom base, the sparkling and shimmer champagne notes and it'll last all day!

## 4. Laugh Often by Reese Witherspoon

![Laugh Often by Reese Witherspoon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/4_laugh-often-by-reese-witherspoon_300x380.jpg)

Who knew that our darling Reese Witherspoon would come up with something oh-so-summery? This amazing scent has some fruity notes to it, some floral and tea notes to it and it's an incredible scent that's ideal for any summer outing! It smells just like the beach along with a bit of spring air. Give it a try!

## 5. Summer by Burberry

![Summer by Burberry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/5_summer-by-burberry_250x250.jpg)

I am in love with most Burberry perfumes, but this limited-edition perfume? It's ideal! It's got some amazingly fresh scent inside, such as pomegranate, orange and green leaf, rose, cedar wood and finally some white musk and sandalwood. It's the perfect perfume to splash out when you're heading out for a day at the beach or on a dinner cruise!

## 6. Flora by Gucci

![Flora by Gucci](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/6_flora-by-gucci_250x250.jpg)

Gucci and I have slowly but surely become friends in the perfume department. I love Envy Me by Gucci, but this is my second favorite. It's a clean scent that smells like citrus, pink pepper, roses, peony (which I love!) and some sandalwood. It's a spontaneous and natural scent!

## 7. L'Eau D'Issey Pour Homme Summer by Issey Miyake

![L'Eau D'Issey Pour Homme Summer by Issey Miyake](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/7_leau-dissey-pour-homme-summer-by-issey-miyake_250x250.jpg)

Now, this is actually a cologne for the guys, but I love to wear it too. It's fruity, but not overpowering and it's got some spice to it too. It's got hints of grapefruit, pineapple, nutmeg, cypress and some coriander. It's a unique scent for you or for your boy!

## 8. One Summer by CK

![One Summer by CK](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/8_one-summer-by-ck_250x250.jpg)

If you're looking for an amazing scent that is full of summer goodness? This is the scent for you! It's full of citrus, watermelon, cucumber, water lily, caraway seeds and finally some musk and moss. It's refreshing, it's crisp and it's oh-so-cool!

So, these are just a couple of my top scents for the summer. What other perfumes or colognes do you wear during the summer? Give 'em up!
