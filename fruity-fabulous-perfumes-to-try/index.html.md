# Fruity Perfumes to Make You Smell Good Enough to Eat ...

With all of the different fruity perfumes out there, it can be hard to choose the right one that is going to work for you! If you've been dying to find the right fruity perfumes that are ideal for the summer, why not take a look below? I've got my top list of perfumes that are super sweet, so fruity and oh-so-delicious!

## 1. Taj Sunset by Escada

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/1_taj-sunset-by-escada_260x396.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

One of my favorite fruity perfumes is actually pretty inexpensive. It smells just like coconut, Malay apple, raspberry, sandalwood and a little bit of musk. It's an amazing scent that smells just like summer and really unfolds when the temperatures heat up! If you want the ultimate fruity perfume, this is it!

## 2. Be Delicious by DKNY

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/3_be-delicious-by-dkny_240x208.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

If you want another amazing fruity perfume, give a try to this juicy fragrance! It’s packed with refreshing and crisp scents including American apple, cucumber, tuberose, white muguet, rose, grapefruit, tender skin accord and some white amber. It's a free-spirited, unique smell that is ideal for any beach bum girl!

## 3. Viva La Juicy by Juicy Couture

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/4_viva-la-juicy-by-juicy-couture_250x250.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

I actually had no idea that this particular perfume packed in so much fruit, until I smelled it! It's got honeysuckle, Mandarins, wild berries, amber, caramel and some amazing vanilla and praline notes to it. It's a teasing, tempting and beautiful scent that you'll be addicted to instantly!

## 4. Fruity Rhythm by Adidas

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/5_fruity-rhythm-by-adidas_461x508.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

This is one of the less expensive fruity options out there and you can get it at drugstores! It's full of raspberry notes, some musk, black currant, sandalwood and even some cyclamen and freesia! I love this incredible scent and it's so inexpensive, I can keep it in stock!

## 5. L'Elixir Eau De Parfum for Women by Nina Ricci

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/6_lelixir-eau-de-parfum-for-women-by-nina-ricci_300x300.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

If you're really looking to amp up the fruity notes in your perfume, this amazing red fruit perfume is ideal for any time in the day! It's packed with jasmine notes, some musk along with some lime and lemon. It's zesty, it's sweet, it's oh-so-fruity and delish!

## 6. RL Pink #2 by Ralph Lauren

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/7_rl-pink-2-by-ralph-lauren_250x250.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

I love that Ralph Lauren has branched out in his perfumes and this amazing perfume smells so good! It's romantic, it's flirty, it's playful and it's oh-so-irresistible! It's packed with cranberry, tonka mousse and some amazing floral scents!

## 7. Bright Crystal by Versace

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/8_bright-crystal-by-versace_248x208.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

Versace, while expensive, has some of the best perfumes out there and this perfume is among them! It's packed with pomegranate, peony, magnolia, lotus flower and some iced accord. It's so fruity, so floral and oh-so-sheer! It's a sensual perfume that you can wear any time!

## 8. Ralph Wild by Ralph Lauren

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/06/9_ralph-wild-by-ralph-lauren_250x250.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

Another from Ralph Lauren, Ralph Wild is packed with cherry blossom fragrance, strawberry, watermelon, pink peppercorn and some red petals and jasmine. It's a sweet scent that isn't overpowering at all!

## 9. Mon Paris by Yves Saint Laurent

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ap/ne/etp51ni359386f4b71b80914781372_600x920.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

Mon Paris evokes the sultry, fruit flavours of the classic French city, with red berries and pears taking centre stage in this wonderful fragrance. It’s fresh, sensual and sophisticated, everything you would expect from a perfume dedicated to Paris!

## 10. Lime Basil and Mandarin by Jo Malone

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cy/ji/ry93j84659386f4b895fb185543930_600x920.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

Get your own fill of the breezy Caribbean with this amazing combination of lime, basil and mandarin. The great thing about this fragrance is that is is equally as great for men as it is women, with none of the notes favouring a particular sex.

## 11. Blooming Bouquet by Miss Dior

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/us/ge/b24gdhg659386f4b7a08b052012852_600x920.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

Though the bouquet name understandably connotes a somewhat floral base, the top note of this fragrance is Italian mandarin essence, giving it a wonderful fruity body that lasts for hours without having to reapply.

## 12. Les Infusions Fleur D’Oranger by Prada

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hp/bs/yfmnd10159386f4bc7fe5024103255_600x920.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

Prada really have created something special here with their fragrance filled with citrus and floral notes. The overpowering mandarin paired with orange blossom makes for an effortlessly summery fruit perfume that can be enjoyed both casually and formally.

## 13. Earl Grey & Cucumber by Jo Malone

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ly/ch/lo9o8lc559386f4c54a38080947286_600x920.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

If you are looking for a perfume that is going to give you an instant hit of classic England, then look no further than this intoxicating fragrance from Jo Malone that combines two essential British notes, Early Grey tea and cucumber!

## 14. My Burberry by Burberry

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ox/or/mmvor34d59386f4c1ca0c197081889_600x920.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

Burberry has really upped their perfume game of late, and this fragrance that boasts notes of lemon and peach makes for wonderfully bright and breezy perfume that can be worn every single day of the summer!

## 15. Beach by Bobbi Brown

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kh/o4/a5ik6vd859386f4f90743964438412_600x920.jpg)

[Source](https://fragrance.allwomenstalk.com/collections/fresh-and-fruity-perfumes/)

It may have the name beach but there are mandarin notes that definitely puts this in the fruity category. It’s light for daytime but intoxicating enough for evening wear when you’re by the sea.

So girls, these are just a few of the amazing fruity scents that I can't get enough of. Do you have any that you love? Do you have any that I missed? Do tell!
