Frequently Asked Questions

Why are vintage perfume bottles so popular?
Oh, aren't they just stunning? People love them because each one is like a little piece of art! They're unique, often handmade, and tell a story from a different era. Plus, they make gorgeous decorations.
How can I verify if my perfume bottle is truly vintage?
Great question! Check for any markings or labels which might give you clues, and consider its design style. If it's from a famous brand, do some research! Sometimes having it appraised by an expert can also be a good idea.
Where can I find antique perfume bottles?
Oh, you have so many options! Try searching online marketplaces, estate sales, or even antique shops. Flea markets can have some hidden gems too if you're up for a little treasure hunt!
What is a vintage Irice perfume bottle?
Irice bottles are a real find! They were known for their ornate and intricate designs. If you spot one, you'll notice they're often beautifully colored glass or with detailed embellishments – they feel quite luxurious!
Why do people collect art deco perfume bottles?
Art deco bottles are all about that fabulous era of glitz and glamour. Collectors love them for their bold designs and geometric shapes. Just having one makes you feel like you've stepped back into the roaring twenties!
