# 55 Gorgeous Vintage Perfume Bottles You'll Lust after ...

I've got a new obsession - vintage perfume bottles! I've always thought glass to be a wonderful artistic medium and have a small collection of antique and vintage drinking glasses. After seeing some of these vintage perfume bottles, I might just have to start a new hobby.

## 1. Green Butterfly

![Green Butterfly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u8/fm/uk4xchua_199x461.jpg)

Via [Tennessee, Antique perfume bottle (breen, ...](https://missingsisterstill.tumblr.com/post/22232800146/antique-perfume-bottle-breen-butterfly)

Many vintage perfume bottles are decanter size with stoppers called daubers. Perfume was used to cool the throat and décolletage, as well as to smell nice. This is a wonderful example of the Art Nouveau style.
