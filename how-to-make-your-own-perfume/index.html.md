# How to Make Your Own Perfume ...

In this video, Laura shows we gals **how to make perfume**!

She demonstrates 4 perfumes using essential oil and fractionated coconut oil or water, utilizing roller bottles and spray bottles.

But, don't just stop there! Play around with the scents! You just might surprise yourself! Make something that makes you feel good!

She even has a link to where you can print out your own perfume bottle labels.

Good for gift-giving and for selling!

Don't have any of the necessities? She has all of them documented above the comments section... What to buy and where to find.

I think I just might look into this myself!

Good luck!
