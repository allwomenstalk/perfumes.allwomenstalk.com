# 9 Innocent & Sweet Perfumes to Try ...

Trying to find innocent and [sweet perfumes](https://perfumes.allwomenstalk.com/deliciously-sweet-perfumes-that-smell-like-candy/) that are ideal for a girl or a woman is not all that easy. I've looked everywhere for innocent and sweet perfumes and I came up with 9 that are amazing! Whether you are looking for designer or you want something a little less expensive, take a look at my list! I promise, I have something for everyone!

## 1. Marc Jacobs – Dot

![Marc Jacobs – Dot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/02/1_marc-jacobs-dot_250x250.jpg)

I actually love, love this scent. Not only is this one of the few innocent and sweet perfumes that is super feminine but it's also not too floral. That's the problem with a lot of sweet perfumes, they can be too floral. This one has vanilla, driftwood and jasmine all mixed and mingled with orange blossom and coconut water. It's an amazing scent!

## 2. Versace – Baby Rose Jeans

![Versace – Baby Rose Jeans](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/02/2_versace-baby-rose-jeans_300x422.jpg)

If you are looking for the ultimate sweet and innocent perfume, this is it. It's not all that heavy and it smells just like heaven! It's got some mandarin orange, violet and freesia notes, along with lily-of-the-valley and vanilla mixed in. It's splendid, girls, especially for teens!

## 3. Thierry Mugler – Innocent

![Thierry Mugler – Innocent](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/02/3_thierry-mugler-innocent_217x384.jpg)

With a name like "Innocent," how could this perfume be anything but innocent? It's a little nutty, a little woody, a little musky and all around a sweet perfume. It's got some beautiful orange notes along with red berries and almond. I love this scent for just how crisp it is!

## 4. Victoria's Secret – Pink

![Victoria's Secret – Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/02/4_victorias-secret-pink_350x350.jpg)

If you are thinking about breaking the norm of floral notes and sweet perfumes, this is it! It's a little spicy but still overall sweet! It's got lily-of-the-valley in it, some green leaves, juniper berry and some incredible peony notes. It's ideal for any girl that isn't too girly!

## 5. Guerlain – My Insolence

![Guerlain – My Insolence](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/02/5_guerlain-my-insolence_248x224.jpg)

If you're looking for another perfume that is ideal for any girl that doesn't want to be too girly, but still wants innocent and sweet – this is it! It's got some Tonka beans, some almond blossoms, some jasmine notes along with some raspberry notes. It's a little citrusy so it's crisp and fresh!

## 6. Calvin Klein – Euphoria

![Calvin Klein – Euphoria](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/02/6_calvin-klein-euphoria_232x192.jpg)

If you're looking for a perfume that is super crisp and super clean, this is the perfume for you! It's filled with pomegranate, persimmon, mahogany wood, black violet and cream accord. This perfume actually unfolds right on your skin!

## 7. Gucci – Envy Me

![Gucci – Envy Me](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/02/7_gucci-envy-me_250x250.jpg)

Before I started writing this list, I actually got a sample of Gucci's Envy Me in the mail and I have to say, it's amazing! It's got some floral notes which are amazing, but then it slips into fruit too. Litchi, pomegranate, pineapple and pink musk are all part of the notes that make up this amazing perfume!

## 8. D&G – the One

![D&G – the One](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/02/8_d-g-the-one_216x208.jpg)

If you're looking for a perfume that is passionate, a perfume that is floral, a perfume that is a distinctive scent, this is the one! It's got vanilla notes sweetened up right beside some ambery musk notes along with some lychee, peach and jasmine. It's a scent for any woman looking to find their perfume!

## 9. Vivienne Westwood – Naughty Alice

![Vivienne Westwood – Naughty Alice](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/02/9_vivienne-westwood-naughty-alice_155x155.jpg)

I actually think that this little scent has the greatest name – and the best smell! Its got black rose, canal violet and has some notes of ylang-ylang to it. It's a beautiful scent that I think that every girl should try at least once!

So girls, these are just a few of my favorite innocent and sweet perfumes! What about you? Do you have any more that you can share? Give 'em up!
