# 10 Delicious Perfumes for Women with a Sweet Tooth ...

You don't want to wear the same perfume every single day for the rest of your life. How boring would that be? Even if you've already found a fragrance you adore, there's always room for some more in your collection. Keeping that in mind, here are a few juicy [perfumes for women](https://allwomenstalk.com/hi/5833684aef250a36298b4568/) with a sweet tooth:

## 1. Paris Hilton Dazzle

![Paris Hilton Dazzle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tx/ke/ulum2xbs57220cbb454c3173090840_500x383.jpg)

Buy it for $20 on [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is my all-time favorite perfume that I wear everywhere I go. I don't care how you feel about Paris Hilton as a person, because she's a genius when it comes to creating perfumes. This one in particular is sweet and fruity. It has notes of cherry, apple, and peach, which gives it a flirty feel. I highly recommend this sweet scent.

## 2. Juicy Couture Viva La Juicy

![Juicy Couture Viva La Juicy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y0/dn/hpx297fg57220cbb5ce6f322904383_500x417.jpg)

Buy it for $74 on [ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is a super [sweet perfume](https://perfumes.allwomenstalk.com/deliciously-sweet-perfumes-that-smell-like-candy/) that smells of caramel. It also has notes of vanilla, orange, and wild berries. It works best for summer days, and since the weather is starting to get warmer, you should think about picking up this perfume as soon as you can. You don't want it to sell out, because it's a popular product that plenty of women love.

## 3. Jessica Simpson Fancy

![Jessica Simpson Fancy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c9/pm/ftd2gtu757220cbb8ed60187909828_500x352.jpg)

Buy it for $10 at [google.com](https://www.google.com/search?q=Jessica+Simpson+Fancy&oq=Jessica+Simpson+Fancy&aqs=chrome..69i57j0l5.174j0j9&sourceid=chrome&ie=UTF-8)

Jessica Simpson knows how to make a profit, because this perfume is pretty amazing. This is a fruity fragrance that smells of caramel and vanilla. It also has notes of red berries, apricots, and pears. You can wear it to the mall during the day and out to the clubs during the night. It works for any occasion.

## 4. Guerlain Spiritueuse Double Vanille

![Guerlain Spiritueuse Double Vanille](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j1/bx/arr9vzw657220cbba7c5f499636225_500x406.jpg)

Buy it for $260 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This perfume has top notes of pink pepper, bergamot and incense, middle notes of ylang-ylang, cedar, bulgarian rose and jasmine, and base notes of benzoin and vanilla. It smells balsamic and slightly spicy, so if you're looking for something that'll shake things up, this is the perfume for you.

## 5. Mirato Malizia Bon Bons Milk Shake

![Mirato Malizia Bon Bons Milk Shake](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bc/jo/g8pj22fn57220cbbd7a72388834474_500x320.jpg)

Buy it for $4 on [amazon.de](http://www.amazon.de?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This smells just like you think it would smell. Like a milk shake! It's milky, sweet, and perfect for young women. If you're still in school, and don't want to buy anything too fancy, then you should try this product. You won't regret it.

## 6. Fantasies Iced Cupcake Fantasy

![Fantasies Iced Cupcake Fantasy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sv/nq/jgucffcj57220cbc6ee7d809006392_500x353.jpg)

Buy it for $10 on [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

You can probably imagine what this perfume smells like, too. It has a sweet, vanilla fragrance with notes of vanilla, cupcake, sugar and musk. It'll make you feel like you're at a fun birthday party every single day! Plus, it's pretty cheap, since it's a body spray and not a traditional perfume.

## 7. Demeter Fragrance Caramel

![Demeter Fragrance Caramel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ij/rg/m0y9fb5f57220cbc790ec104926015_500x359.jpg)

Buy it for $22 on [fragrancex.com](http://www.fragrancex.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is a sweet, caramel scent that feels rich and syrupy. If you love to eat the gooey dessert, then you're going to love smelling like it all day long. Of course, it's probably going to make you hungry, so if you're on a diet, you might want to stay away from this one.

## 8. Prada Candy Eau De Parfum Spray

![perfume, cosmetics, glass bottle, brand, bottle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/la/j5/hio62lxc5947403ac065e159122576_522x522.jpg)

Buy it for $73 on [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

As the name implies, this delicious perfume is packed with notes of vanilla and caramel, reminiscent of your favorite piece of candy. Not only does this perfume smell really tasty, but it will last all day long so you'll never have to worry about it fading by lunchtime.

## 9. Parfurms De Coeur Cotton Candy Fantasies

![perfume, lotion, product, cosmetics, skin care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rn/pn/jseccts5594740e978080217865001_500x679.jpg)

Buy it for $6 at [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You're never going to find a more affordable perfume! If you love cotton candy, you are going to adore this lovely scent. It's pink and delicious and will be your signature scent in no time at all.

## 10. Ariana Grande Sweet like Candy Eau De Parfum Spray

![pink, perfume, product, petal, cosmetics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fp/nx/dj81x8sf5947415cb4578177438660_522x392.jpg)

Buy it for $30 on [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The packaging for this glorious perfume is so wonderful and it only accentuates how delicious the candy scented perfume smells. Blackberry and bergamot are the main ingredients that give this perfume its yummy smell, but you also get notes of vanilla and marshmallow. You're going to love it!

Now you have plenty of perfume options for the next time you go out on a big date or attend a fun party. What's your favorite, sweet-smelling perfume?
