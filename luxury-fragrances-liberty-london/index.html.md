## The Art of Scent: Crafted with Passion

When it comes to designing a fragrance, Liberty London spares no effort in crafting perfection. Collaborating with some of the industry's most talented noses, they blend exquisite perfumes with the elegance of English craftsmanship. Each candle is hand-poured in England, made with soy and coconut wax to ensure a clean, longer-lasting burn. The careful layering of scents with botanicals and iconic prints creates not just a product but an experience - a journey into the realms of luxury and fine art.

### **Soy & Coconut Wax:**

Soy and coconut wax are known for being environmentally friendly compared to traditional paraffin. Beyond their eco-friendliness, these materials offer a slow and even burn, enhancing the fragrance's dispersion.
