# 7 New Perfumes for Men ...

Fresh and clean, classy and masculine or warm and deep with just a hint of floral – there’s like a truck load of great new perfumes for men this year! A good perfume is something we can never cross from our list of potential valentine’s day/birthday/Christmas gifts, partially because we like our men to smell the way we want them to and partially because they know that and some of them are even willing to give us the complete and total freedom in choosing the fragrance for them to wear on daily basis. And here are 7 new perfumes for men you might want to check out in case you have some gift-shopping to do soon:

## 1. Bvlgari Aqua Pour Homme Toniq

![Bvlgari Aqua Pour Homme Toniq](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p7/w0/lqbkpgf954c927bb30150077334245_200x184.jpg)

**Price:** $79.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If icy, fresh, aquatic scents are your man’s all time favorites, check out this new, limited edition version of Bvlgari Aqua. It’s actually the first of two brand new perfumes for men introduced by this name which means you’ll have a choice between this one and a slightly woodier “Aqua Marine Toniq” version.

## 2. Jean Paul Gaultier Le Male Summer

![Jean Paul Gaultier Le Male Summer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/la/or/lhx72brx54c927bbe39ab447124660_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $59.00 at [sephora.com](https://goo.gl/eq25hF)
Recognizable male torso bottle returns slightly different this summer and I must say I’m totally diggin’ the Maori tattoos! Now, if the bottle alone is not enough to convince you that this is THE one for your beloved man (and I believe the bottle alone is rarely the one to make us decide to buy a perfume), here’s what this interesting, refreshing new scent is all about – orange blossom, lavender, mint, musk, vanilla and, of course, sandalwood and cardamom.

## 3. Prada Amber Pour Homme Intense

![Prada Amber Pour Homme Intense](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yg/y0/xq2mnmrg54c927bcb21a7257224720_163x238.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 - $75.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Woody, leathery and definitely classically masculine – I doubt any of the other new perfumes for men smells anything like this one! Now, I do have a very strict policy on leather and regardless of how much I like the smell of new shoes and bags, I just don’t like it in perfumes. That, of course, doesn’t mean it’s bad so do check it out. Smelling it doesn’t mean you have to buy it and you never know… some people really like it!

## 4. YSL L’Homme

![YSL L’Homme](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sn/r4/ct15f6mf54c927bd6daaf444824281_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $43.00 - $95.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I haven’t checked it out yet but the combination of ginger, bergamot, white pepper and cedar made it rank really high on my must-smell list, so if this sounds like something you’d gladly smell every day, do stop by your local Sephora. YSL perfumes are one of my favorite ones so hope my fiancé will like this one – then we could be YSL fans together 

## 5. Gucci Guilty Pour Homme

![Gucci Guilty Pour Homme](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pu/rv/qk7ak1si54c927be10984544603508_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $57.00 - $73.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Interesting, fresh and playful but, wait a bit and you’ll discover warm, deep notes you didn’t see coming. Now that definitely sounds like my man! I’ve heard a lot of both positive and negative opinions about this new Gucci fragrance but I simply refuse to let them affect mine! Call me predictable but I still love it. Plus, my man likes it so that pretty much settles it.

## 6. L'Eau D'Issey Pour Homme Summer

![L'Eau D'Issey Pour Homme Summer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ah/n5/fdkgrcik54c927bec3666078591522_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $59.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
In case none of these new perfumes for men sounded like something you’d like to be around every day, here’s one fresh, limited edition fragrance that just might tickle your nostrils. This interesting bottle holds an even more interesting composition of mandarin, cardamom, sage, vetiver and ambergris.

## 7. L'Eau Par Kenzo Pour Homme Wild Edition

_ **Price:** $55.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Coriander, mint, cedar and, surprisingly, no vetiver! This Kenzo perfume is everything you’d expect a summer fragrance to be – fresh, energizing and modern. It’s made for outgoing, cool guys who like to keep things interesting. Is this the summer fragrance you’ve been looking for all along? Maybe… you never know.

Have you smelled some of these perfumes and, if the answer is yes, what do you have to say about them? Now, in case none of them was good enough for you, I’d just have to advise you to sit tight as many more new perfumes for men are yet about to hit the stores in July, August and September.
