# Non\-Basic Perfumes You'll Want to Wear All Week Long ...

Perfume is something that I absolutely love! I love the fresh, clean scents. The fruity and the floral. If you're looking for non-basic perfumes to wear this winter, we got you covered! Take a look at my top 17 perfumes for the non-basic girl!

![Miu Miu Eau De Parfum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k6/vy/mjdzc1g45685f78ccac8d457868060_400x456.jpg)

Get it here: [neimanmarcus.com](http://www.neimanmarcus.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Nicki Minaj the PinkPrint

![Nicki Minaj the PinkPrint](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/af/kc/de09g17f5685f78ebe516570733074_466x382.jpg)

## 4. Marc Jacobs Decadence Eau De Parfum

![Marc Jacobs Decadence Eau De Parfum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q6/to/y9jg3mh25685f79328b84074430206_600x338.jpg)

## 5. Estée Lauder Modern Muse Le Rouge

![Estée Lauder Modern Muse Le Rouge](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dx/oa/dyysu59f5685f79139d8e021032339_600x404.jpg)

Get it here: [macys.com](http://www1.macys.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Donna Karan Liquid Black Cashmere Eau De Parfum

![Donna Karan Liquid Black Cashmere Eau De Parfum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s8/c3/u54jejl55685f78f65222913181348_600x729.jpg)

Get it here: [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 7. Diptyque Essences Insensées Eau De Parfum

![Diptyque Essences Insensées Eau De Parfum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o9/r8/tmwprwaf5685f794d616d150705824_600x529.jpg)

Oh yes, this scent is perfect for anyone looking for a simple yet beautiful perfume. It's got notes of jasmine, orange blossom flower and basil and beautifully unfolds!

Get it here: [amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 8. Chloé Eau De Toilette

_ This is another woody perfume, but it's lighter and pure. It's long lasting too, which is something that I absolutely love. It's perfect for anyone that is looking for a playful perfume!

Get it here: beautyspin.com_

## 9. Aesop Tacit Eau De Parfum

![Aesop Tacit Eau De Parfum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xs/bv/ip8x6u345685f79144e83686264902_600x363.jpg)

Although this an expensive perfume, it's worth it! It is packed with basil, Grand Vert and fresh, fresh notes. It's clean, beautiful and I absolutely love it!

Get it here: [aesop.com](http://www.aesop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 10. Bvlgari Omnia Paraiba

![Bvlgari Omnia Paraiba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yj/b2/wmknxb4i5685f7926389d828280459_500x272.jpg)

Passion fruit, passion flower, orange and cocoa beans are the top notes in this beautiful perfume!

Get it here: [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 11. Shay & Blue Framboise Noire Natural Fragrance Spray

![Shay & Blue Framboise Noire Natural Fragrance Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xr/pb/xnafwbnu5685f7950bb16198309822_600x564.jpg)

You want a fruity perfume? This is it! It has notes of black and red berries with a heart of iris and base notes of musk and dark woods.

Get it here: [johnlewis.com](https://bit.ly/1nfm9Ky)

## 12. Yves Saint Laurent Black Opium Eau De Toilette

![Yves Saint Laurent Black Opium Eau De Toilette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zs/k5/gn6ykkhf5685f795e24fe979711835_600x583.jpg)

This perfume has a cool bottle as well as it smells awesome! Fruity top notes of citrus and blackcurrant introduce a heart of coffee, jasmine, orange blossom and tea, whilst white musk and white woods are the base.

Get it here: [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 13. Dolce & Gabbana Velvet Mimosa Bloom

_ Another expensive one, but it's so worth it! This scent is dominated by Mimosa blossoms; Mandarin, Bergamot and Neroli oil evoke a warm Mediterranean Spring.

Get it here: barneys.com_

## 14. NARCISO Eau De Toilette

![NARCISO Eau De Toilette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rq/cx/q8379fro5685f79641adf673634515_500x194.jpg)

Want woods? Want flowers? This is the beautiful perfume for you! It's got notes of sensual woods, Bulgarian rose and white peony. So not basic!

Get it here: [bloomingdales.com](http://www1.bloomingdales.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 15. VILHELM PARFUMERIE Smoke Show Eau De Parfum
