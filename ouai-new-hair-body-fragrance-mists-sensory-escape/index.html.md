# OUAI’s New Hair & Body Fragrance Mists Are Your Next Great Sensory Escape

**There are two types of travelers in this world.**

The ones who actually board flights, neatly pack their 3.3-ounce liquids into clear zip bags, glide through terminals with silk scarves fluttering in the wind…

And then there are the rest of us, more often than not bound for Boca to visit the grandparents, traveling far-way lands aspirationally, fueled by caffeine, a questionable self-tan, and the deep, unwavering solace of beauty products that take us somewhere, even if our demanding work schedules cannot.

Fortunately for both demographics, **OUAI has launched what may be the chicest shortcut to a holiday abroad since duty-free champagne**. [Meet OUAI’s new Hair & Body Fragrance Mists](https://theouai.com/products/santorini-hair-and-body-fragrance-mist), two transportive scent experiences inspired by the most mood-defining coordinates on the map: **sunset-to-sunrise Ibiza and fresh-salted Santorini**.

**Lightweight yet lasting, sensual yet wearable on both hair and skin**, these mists are small enough to toss into a handbag yet big enough in personality to announce you long before your heels hit the floor. **Both fragrances landed December 8th for Sephora App preview and December 9th at TheOuai.com & Sephora.com, priced at a delightful $28 for 3.3 fl oz** - proof that luxury escapism does not require a private jet (though it pairs beautifully with one, should that be your situation).

What OUAI has essentially bottled here is **vacation energy**: the confidence of golden shoulders, the sweetness of late nights, the lightness of linen sheets drying in the open air. Consider this your boarding pass.

Choose your destination below - no passport required.

## 1. ** Ibiza: The Mist for Women Who Want More Than Just a Good Time**

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/google-doc-ref-image1-1765319104737.png)

[IBIZA HAIR & BODY FRAGRANCE MIST](https://theouai.com/products/ibiza-hair-and-body-fragrance-mist)

**Price: $28 (3.3 fl oz)**

**Availability: December 8th (Sephora App Preview), December 9th (TheOuai.com & Sephora.com)**

There are nights you sip sparkling water and scroll real estate listings, telling yourself we are budgeting, we are sensible, we are grounded.

And then there are Ibiza nights.

OUAI’s new Ibiza Hair & Body Fragrance Mist is the latter in bottled form - a warm, gourmand-leaning scent made for women who deserve the table with the best view, who answer “sparkling? still?” with “champagne.” It is a fragrance that whispers seduction but laughs easily, a scent built for evening humidity, glowing shoulders, and the sinful pleasure of never checking the time.

### **What’s inside this little sunset-to-sunrise miracle?**

A heady combination of sugar, cocoa, and tonka bean greets you first - warming, decadent, just shy of edible but still undeniably elegant. The heart blooms into labdanum, coffee, and cashmeran, adding depth and silk to the composition, the fragrance equivalent of sliding into a perfectly broken-in leather booth at 1 a.m. And then - when things start to get interesting - vanilla, cinnamon, and rum carry you through like the final song on rotation that you absolutely refuse to let be the last.

If scents had dress codes, Ibiza is sequins with bare skin, bronzer applied where the light hits, and the kind of effortless hair you only pretend wasn’t styled on purpose.

### **It smells like:**

• Sunset to sunrise

• Dancing the night a-OUAI

• You have the best table at the beach club

You may not speak fluent Spanish, but you will speak main character.

### **How to use it**

Mist generously - on hair, on collarbones, on that soft pulse point behind your knee you didn’t know was a pulse point until this precise moment. OUAI encourages application whenever you want to get-a-OUAI (their words, though I’d like them embroidered on a robe I can dramatically shrug off at dinner).

### **Formula perks**

Color safe. Cruelty-free. Sustainable. Paraben-free. Vegan. Recyclable.

In essence: a good time with a clean conscience.

With this scent, you are not at Ibiza - you are Ibiza. The heat, the music, the neon-lit glamour of sensibility discarded in favor of pleasure and peach-tinted skies. Wear it for first kisses, last trains, and the kind of evenings you’ll reference for years as “that night when everything felt possible.”

Some fragrances are worn. Ibiza is lived.

## 2. ** Santorini: Sunlight, Salt, Skin—A Mist for Women Who Wake Up Smiling**

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/google-doc-ref-image2-1765319106398.png)

[SANTORINI HAIR & BODY FRAGRANCE MIST](https://theouai.com/products/santorini-hair-and-body-fragrance-mist)

**Price: $28 (3.3 fl oz)**

**Availability: December 8th (Sephora App Preview), December 9th (TheOuai.com & Sephora.com)**

If Ibiza is the midnight provocateur, Santorini is the exhale - the cool sheet morning after, the bright impossibly blue horizon, the feeling that life is gentle and we are lucky to be here.

This fresh & citrusy hair-to-toe mist is sunshine laced with sea breeze and ripe fruit markets, made for women whose idea of luxury is slow breakfast under bougainvillea and the sound of waves approving their decisions.

### **The notes you’ll notice first**

A sparkling trio of peach, orange, and grapefruit - the olfactory equivalent of golden hour illuminating white stone villas. It is juicy without being sticky, radiant rather than sweet. Soon after, honeysuckle, berries, and rose expand the scent into floral terrain, the kind you can only describe as vacation skin. Finally, vanilla, amber, and musk leave a soft lasting trail, warm like sun-kissed shoulders and breeze-dried cotton.

Where Ibiza leans toward seduction, Santorini leans toward serenity. It is not quiet - it’s harmonious.

### **It smells like:**

• Diving all the OUAI in

• You’re on top of the world

• Soaking up the seabreeze

This is the fragrance for women who pause to savor their morning coffee.

Who return from trips with sand in their luggage, sea salt in their hair, and a brand-new vow to slow down - at least for the next three weeks.

### **When to mist**

Anytime you want to feel like the sun is warm, the ocean is close, and you’ve chosen well. OUAI suggests spraying all over hair and body whenever you wanna get-a-OUAI - helpfully vague, enthusiastically permissive.

It’s beach air without boarding a plane. It’s summer running down your spine in December. It is a reason to smile at strangers in crosswalks because you smell like someone who just got back from somewhere beautiful.

And at $28? It feels illegal not to.

## 3. ** The Beauty of Duality: Why Two Fragrance Mists Matter More Than One**

![](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/google-doc-ref-image3-1765319107237.png)

Fragrance, at its best, isn’t decoration - it’s identity architecture. It tells the world who we are today, not forever. And OUAI understands the modern woman is not one note. She is soft one morning and untamed by nightfall; she is sea-salt delicate and rum-burning bold, often simultaneously.

So perhaps the real brilliance of this launch isn’t that these mists smell exquisite - though they do. It’s that OUAI has given us choice. A reversible postcard. A mood wardrobe.

Do you want to dance at midnight like the universe owes you joy?

Choose Ibiza.

Do you want to inhale sunlight and feel like the world is kind?

Choose Santorini.

Or - and here lies the quiet genius - layer them.

Begin with Santorini, fresh and fruit-bright as morning. Add Ibiza at pulse points, creamy with rum and vanilla, like night blooming early beneath the skin. Suddenly, you’re living 24 hours at once: sunrise on your shoulders, sunset at your throat. A woman who contains multitudes. A woman who does not choose.
