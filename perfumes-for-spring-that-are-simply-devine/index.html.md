# 7 Perfumes for Spring That Are Simply Divine ...

When it comes to perfumes for spring, I definitely won't hesitate in saying that there are plenty of contenders. One of my favorite parts about hunting for old and new perfumes is falling on hidden treasures. Now that the bitter cold is almost gone, rocking freshly scented perfumes on fun outings is at the top of my list. These flawless perfumes for spring are just a few that seem to capture the very essence of this beautiful season!

## 1. French Lavender and Honey

![French Lavender and Honey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j7/dh/a7mljvs6_600x654.jpg)

Bath and Body Works' new French Lavender and Honey signature collection is certainly one of the sweetest smelling perfumes for spring. From the smooth shower gel to the phenomenally scented fragrance mist, everything about this collection reminds me of warm and sunshine-filled days. I'm typically not a huge fan of honey but even I couldn't resist this intriguing blend. The fact that this collection is also infused with shea and Vitamin E is the icing on the cake.

## 2. Honeymania

![Honeymania](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hz/f0/vidghzkv_600x277.jpg)

One of my biggest mottos when it comes to shopping for a new fragrance is if it's inexpensive and smells great, then it clearly belongs in my home. Once again, I find myself reevaluating my initial aversion to honey (both scents and in its natural form); with every spritz of the Body Shop's fresh Honeymania eau de toilette, my opinion was changed further.

## 3. Sweet Pea Eau De Toilette

![Sweet Pea Eau De Toilette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k2/j6/tgvpl18e_600x743.jpg)

From its sophisticated design to the intoxicating smell, Sweet Pea eau de toilette from Bath and Body Works deserves to be in constant rotation. I didn't think it was possible for me to fall further in love with sweet pea notes; it's safe to say that I don't regret trying it for a single moment and I'm positive that you won't either!

## 4. Victoria Eau De Parfum

![Victoria Eau De Parfum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fk/t9/yqgmygtr_600x738.jpg)

Containing a mix of red berry and Victoria rose notes, don't be surprised if this floral eau de parfum ends up on your radar. I love how sensual this perfume makes me feel (even on the days that I'm running simple errands). I only wish the fragrance was stronger so I could wear this every day!

## 5. Tocca Beauty Bagno Da Viaggio

![Tocca Beauty Bagno Da Viaggio](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i6/pz/vqd5o1ps_450x448.jpg)

This set from Sephora includes a spicy infusion of blood orange, white freesia, wild diamond orchids and spicy lily (among other sexy notes). Not only does using the body wash, lotion and eau de parfum together promote fragrance longevity, they are also subtle enough to catch a stranger's attention without being overpowering.

## 6. Tokyomilk Dark Tempting Trio

![Tokyomilk Dark Tempting Trio](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/at/r5/obbgg2oy_450x383.jpg)

Tokyomilk Dark's Tempting Trio certainly lives up to its name. Although small in size, the smooth application of the eau de parfum rollerball and cream makes up for it. Both fruity and floral scent lovers will get a kick out of the daring mix of sparkling citrus, dark vanilla bean, and sandalwood notes. If you are looking for a product that is both moisturizing, soothing and can fit in your purse, you don't need to look any further.

## 7. Body by Victoria Eau De Parfum

![Body by Victoria Eau De Parfum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xy/by/qn0bcq0y_600x456.jpg)

Lastly, the revision of this spring-inspired perfume definitely makes me happy that the season is here. I really love how well the freesia and water hyacinth blend maintains a perfect balance between each note.

Now that spring is in full swing, the promise of warmer days is a great excuse to go on a search for amazing perfumes. What are your favorite places to drop by? What perfumes would you recommend are worth trying?
