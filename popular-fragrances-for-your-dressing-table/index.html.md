# 8 Popular Fragrances for Your Dressing Table ...

Popular Fragrances vary in smell and design. There are a wide range of perfumes available, but only a select few reach dizzying heights of popularity. Whether this comes down to smell or successful marketing is something else to consider. However, there’s no denying that some popular fragrances look too good not to own. I love it when my perfume looks just as good as it smells. The following are just a few popular fragrances that not only smell great, but look great as well.

## 1. Chanel ‘No. 5’

Chanel No.5 is ne of the most popular fragrances in the world. It is a timeless classic and a fashion icon in itself. Created by perfume designer Ernest Beaux for Coco Chanel, the perfume is definitive of all things feminine and luxe. It also makes for a chic decoration for your dressing table.

## 2. Dior ‘Miss Dior’

This fragrance has stood the test of time. It was originally launched in 1947, but was reinvented in 2005. The latter version has proved to be hugely popular. The newer version is a lighter take on the floral fragrance, with cherry and caramel tones.

## 3. Marc Jacobs ‘Daisy’

Daisy is marketed as a floral perfume with a vintage edge. It’s a popular fragrance that also comes in a whimsical bottle, complete with a daisy decorated lid. It has quickly become one of the more covetable perfumes on the market.

## 4. Viktor & Rolf ‘Flowerbomb’

Flowerbomb was the first fragrance for Viktor & Rolf and was released in 2005 in conjunction with L’Oreal. This fragrance is housed in a grenade-shaped bottle, which looks rather delicate and elegant. When it comes to popular fragrances, this is probably one of the modern classics.

## 5. Chanel ‘Coco Mademoiselle’

Another Chanel perfume, Coco Mademoiselle is a lighter and more youthful take on the ‘Coco’ fragrance. It was introduced in 2001 with a younger audience in mind. It’s no surprise then that models and celebrities such as Kate Moss and Keira Knightley have been used in recent marketing campaigns.

## 6. ‘Chloe’ by Chloe

In the same vein of Miss Dior and Daisy, Chloe by Chloe is another example of a popular modern fragrance. It is a feminine and youthful scent, made even more covetable by its gorgeous bottle with ribbon detail.

## 7. Ralph Lauren ‘Romance’

Everything about this particular fragrance exemplifies the idea of romance. From the lush fragrance to the sleek glass bottle and pastel pink packaging, this perfume was destined to become one of the more popular fragrances around.

## 8. Vera Wang ‘Princess’

This sweet and floral fragrance is encased in a whimsical heart-shaped bottle. Staying true to the ‘princess’ theme, it also has a crown-shaped lid. This fresh scent is perfect for wearing during the day.

These are just a few popular fragrances that are available at the moment. Personally, I wouldn’t mind having any of these popular fragrances adorning my dressing table! Have you tried any of these particular fragrances?
