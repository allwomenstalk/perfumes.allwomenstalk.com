# 9 Seductive Perfumes That You Should Try ...

With all of the different seductive perfumes out there, it's hard to find one that will not just stick to your skin, but that will lure the man of your dreams in! If you've been dying to find all of the right seductive perfumes that'll make you irresistible to any guy, take a look at my list! I've developed the top 9 perfumes that are super seductive and oh-so-beautiful!

## 1. Tommy Girl by Tommy Hilfiger

![Tommy Girl by Tommy Hilfiger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/05/1_tommy-girl-by-tommy-hilfiger_410x410.jpg)

This is one of the best seductive perfumes because it is also innocent … and amazing on any skin! It was developed in 1996 and it's floral and fruity, all at the same time! It's got notes of black currant, lily, lemon, rose and mint – all packed into a perfume that will lure your man in!

## 2. Lovely by Sarah Jessica Parker

![Lovely by Sarah Jessica Parker](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/05/2_lovely-by-sarah-jessica-parker_410x410.jpg)

Who would've thought that SJP would come up with a perfume that is subtle yet musky, that is appealing and oh-so-sexy? It's the perfect balance of bright and fresh, the ideal notes of lavender, apple and amber. It's a great perfume that unfolds and doesn't overpower at all!

## 3. Shalimar by Guerlain

![Shalimar by Guerlain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/05/3_shalimar-by-guerlain_250x250.jpg)

If you are looking for the ultimate seductive perfume, you've got to take a look at this amazing fragrance! It's bright, it's beautiful and it's warm. It's got notes of lemon, rose, iris, tonka bean and vanilla. It's a beautiful scent that is going to capture any guy instantly!

## 4. Jasmin Noir by Bvlgari

![Jasmin Noir by Bvlgari](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/05/4_jasmin-noir-by-bvulgari_250x250.jpg)

Another woody little perfume that is all about seduction, all about refined elegance and all about sophistication is this amazing Bvulgari perfume! It's all about the green sap, gardenia petals, satiny almond, precious wood, licorice and tonka bean. It's a sweet scent that is so, so beautiful once it's on your skin!

## 5. Bombshell by Victoria Secret

![Bombshell by Victoria Secret](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/05/5_bombshell-by-victoria-secret_300x257.jpg)

Victoria's Secret has come up with some of the most amazing perfumes in the world, but this one? It's all about the fruit, all about the sweetness and all about the sex. I actually love the way this incredible fragrance stays put too – it's awesome!

## 6. Guilty by Gucci

![Guilty by Gucci](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/05/6_guilty-by-gucci_250x250.jpg)

I actually just got to smell this a few days ago and instantly, I knew that this was going to be one of the most seductive perfumes that I've ever smelled! It's a beautifully floral and [fruity perfume](https://perfumes.allwomenstalk.com/fruity-fabulous-perfumes-to-try/) that has all sorts of citrus hints, amber notes, patchouli splashes and little hints of black pepper. Such a great smell!

## 7. Flowerbomb by Viktor & Rolf

![Flowerbomb by Viktor & Rolf](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/05/7_flowerbomb-by-viktor-rolf_250x250.jpg)

Flowerbomb is another beautiful scent that is so, so seductive, it'll drive any man crazy! It's a beautiful scent that is all about the floral notes! It's got some amazing cattleya orchids, ballerina freesia, sambac jasmine and finally patchouli notes to it.

## 8. Chanel No. 5 by Chanel

![Chanel No. 5 by Chanel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/05/8_chanel-no-5-by-chanel_195x244.jpg)

If you've never, ever tried Chanel No. 5, now is the time to do it. Not only is this perfume one of the most seductive out there, but it's a classic, it's iconic, it's so, so beautiful on your skin! This is the ultimate sexy perfume and when you slip into this scent, he'll be after you in no time at all!

## 9. Hypnotic Poison by Dior

![Hypnotic Poison by Dior](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2013/05/9_hypnotic-poison-by-dior_250x250.jpg)

Finally, the last perfume that we're going to talk about that is super seductive is bewitching, it's Dior, it's spicy. This perfume is full of bitter almond, cinnamon, tuberose, musk, vanilla and even some amazing woody notes. It's an amazing scent!

So girls, these are just a few of the seductive scents that I have in my perfume collection, do you have any more that you can share? What perfumes do you wear when you want to feel sexy?
