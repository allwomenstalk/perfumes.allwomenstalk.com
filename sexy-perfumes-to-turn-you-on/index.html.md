# 8 Sexy Perfumes to Turn You on ...

**Sexy perfumes aren't that difficult** to find – if you know the scent that you're looking for. Ladies, I love perfume and I've got the top 8 sexy perfumes that will not only turn you on, but that are ideal for date night! So if you have a date coming up and you're looking to smell sexy, take a look at my top 8 sexy perfumes that'll keep your man close!

## 1. Very Sexy for Her by Victoria's Secret

![Very Sexy for Her by Victoria's Secret](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/03/1_very-sexy-for-her-by-victorias-secret_274x300.jpg)

**Price:** $67.32 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Victoria's Secret really knows sexy and I gotta say, this is one **of my favorite sexy perfumes** out there! The notes are a little bit sharp, but they are incredible! This perfume smells like pepper, a little flowery and is definitely musky. It also lasts a really long time, so you don't have to worry about it wearing off before date night is over!

![Lust Perfume by Sex in the City](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/03/2_lust-perfume-by-sex-in-the-city_300x179.jpg)

**Price:** $2.50 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I gotta say, Sexy in the City knows everything about sex. If you've ever watched the show, you know that this show really tells the truth about what is sexy and what isn't. This incredible sexy perfume is not only a bottle of sex, but it smells awesome! Musky and sweet and hot!

## 3. Bright Crystal Perfume by Versace

![Bright Crystal Perfume by Versace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/03/3_bright-crystal-perfume-by-versace_226x277.jpg)

**Price:** $6.39 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The fragrance of this perfume is a different kind of sexy than all of the ones above, it's actually more innocent. It's got a fresh and crisp floral scent to it. It also is a classy perfume that still has a sexy side to it!

## 4. Young Sexy Lovely Perfume by Yves Saint Laurent

![Young Sexy Lovely Perfume by Yves Saint Laurent](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/03/4_young-sexy-lovely-perfume-by-yves-saint-laurent_300x183.jpg)

**Price:** $39.50 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I actually think that this YSL perfume is so light, so sexy and so floral! Not only does this sexy perfume have a soft floral scent to it, but it's a perfume that will your man smelling you for days! It is also so light that it can be worn all day, which is a huge plus!

## 5. Little Things Love Rock by Victoria's Secret

![Little Things Love Rock by Victoria's Secret](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/03/5_little-things-love-rock-by-victorias-secret_300x300.jpg)

**Price:** $21.89 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I actually love the bottle on this perfume! It's hot, sexy and adorable! So ladies, want to know what this sexy perfume smells like? It's got some fruity and flirty little notes at the start, but it's also got some honeysuckle and jasmine in the undertones. Pear, green orange and peach follow later. This is absolutely one of the sexiest perfumes I've ever smelled!

## 6. Tommy Girl EDC by Tommy Hilfiger

![Tommy Girl EDC by Tommy Hilfiger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/03/6_tommy-girl-edc-by-tommy-hilfiger_135x300.jpg)

**Price:** $17.45 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love Tommy Hilfiger products, especially the perfumes and I gotta say that this perfume is no different! It's got a unique and different smell to it and it's actually light enough that you can wear it every single day. So ladies, are you gonna be a Tommy Girl?

## 7. Wonderstruck by Taylor Swift

![Wonderstruck by Taylor Swift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/03/7_wonderstruck-by-taylor-swift_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $52.50 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Taylor Swift is one of those girls that knows lust, knows love and knows what it's like to be sexy. That's why she came out with Wonderstruck. A perfume that smells so sexy, so innocent and so awesome! It's got some fruit scents, some floral scents and a swirl of vibrancy!

## 8. Rose the One by D&G

![Rose the One by D&G](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/perfumes/2012/03/8_rose-the-one-by-d-g_300x164.jpg)

**Price:** $61.42 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Finally, if you're looking for something that is a little more on the romantic side, this is the perfume for you! It's got a hint of rose, some floral scents and also some lychee, lily and some peony. Trust me, this is the perfume for the woman that wants romance in her life!
