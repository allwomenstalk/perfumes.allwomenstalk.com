# Sexy Scents for Summer Nights Men Cannot Resist ...

I think there’s a big difference between a perfume you’d pick for a warm summer’s day and one you want for a sultry evening. I’ve always chosen a heavily sensuous, spicy, oriental perfume for balmy summer nights, but that’s not for everyone. If you’re looking for a new summer night’s scent, maybe it’s lingering among these:

## 1. Original by Anja Rubik Eau De Parfum

![Original by Anja Rubik Eau De Parfum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ub/kp/b0smc9z555d494bd6dfa5940128074_600x613.jpg)

An unusual and original choice, this wonderful perfume by Anja Rubik utilizes notes of pink peppercorn and green tea to create a fragrance that is slightly heavier and headier than the sweet scents that dominate the market. This innovative combination is the perfect accompaniment on a hazy, sultry summer night date.

Buy at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/oRKbrW)

## 2. The Taste of Fragrance Angel

![The Taste of Fragrance Angel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ri/u7/pbr13bsi55d494bb7ece8559251597_300x296.jpg)

If you love Thierry Mugler’s Angel but want something deeper and more seductive, then this edition is perfect. Described as a scent that unites the worlds of perfume and haute cuisine, this perfume stirs the sensuous soul with chocolate and caramel, bergamot and passion fruit.

Buy at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Eau De Parfum Lègere

![Eau De Parfum Lègere](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gj/lw/klz6x6j455d494bd04cd8286352309_550x557.jpg)

One of the best sexy summer scents around, Eau de Parfum Lègere boasts an irresistible combination of sweet floral and much deeper, headier, velvety fragrances that perfectly encapsulate the feelings and emotions of an intense summer romance. Rich and slightly sweet at the same time, a sure fire winner!

Buy at [cartier.us](https://goo.gl/APXxWT)

## 4. Walk on Air Eau De Parfum

![Walk on Air Eau De Parfum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hp/ig/h0hjiash55d494bca29b6686685021_500x467.jpg)

As the name of the fragrance might suggest, the confidence that spritzing some of this scent can bring will do wonders for making you completely irresistible to your man. The number one choice of all Kate Spade fans, the fresh, summery notes that are contained within Walk on Air will help you to feel that true summer feeling, and I can guarantee that your man will love it just as much as you do!

Buy at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. L’Eau De Chloe Eau De Toilette Spray

![L’Eau De Chloe Eau De Toilette Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zk/wm/nospzbdk55d494be4a6ae932016827_600x597.jpg)

Though this perfume possesses a number of the sweeter, more floral notes that are typical in many summer scents, what makes it something special are the warm, deep, woody notes that are brought by the hints of cedar wood and amber that are present. In the sticky heat of the summer, the heavier and headier your fragrance is, the less able your man will be to resist your charms!

Buy at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Versace Eros

![Versace Eros](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zp/b0/j782pgsc55d494be8d0ed311509758_600x894.jpg)

Do you want to evoke the smells of a vacation on a Mediterranean coast? This smells exactly of what you’d expect with the presence of the God of Love in thrown in for good measure. Crisp and summery with just the right touch of opulence and seduction, lemon, bergamot, jasmine and sandalwood will have you and your man dreaming of moonlight walks on a Greek Island beach or in an olive grove in Italy.

Buy at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 7. Korres White Tea Bergamot Freesia

_ If woody, heady perfumes are not your thing, then a wonderful alternative is this White Tea Bergamot Freesia. Effortlessly elegant, the clean and refreshing notes of this perfume are not as sickly sweet as some that you can buy, but they do just as fantastic a job of evoking everything that we love about the summer. Some have described the perfume as being a garden party in a bottle, and hey, summer garden parties can be just as romantic as the thought of a warm beach date or a hazy stroll through the woods!

Buy at korres.com_
