# 7 Sexy Scents to Buy Your Boyfriend ...

You're not the only one in the relationship who should be walking around while smelling delicious. Your boyfriend should have a scent of his own that makes him smell irresistible. Since he might not buy cologne on his own (because it can get pretty pricy), it's your job to pick some up for him. If you can't figure out which one is the best for him, try buying one of these sexy scents:

## 1. Escape for Men

![Escape for Men](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/py/sr/l8o3fsrt56322ae57d075019790886_250x250.jpg)

$22 on [fragrancenet.com](http://www.fragrancenet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This fragrance contains birch leaf, grapefruit, clary sage, cypress, patchouli and vetiver. It's one of a kind, but it's perfect for him to wear during the day or at night. If you absolutely love this scent, then there's a female version of it that's pretty similar. Then you and your man can match!

## 2. Aqua Di Gio

![Aqua Di Gio](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b2/rg/mo2169g856322ae61c2b1525446695_327x400.jpg)

$35 at [macys.com](http://www1.macys.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is a fragrance that mixes together aquatic scents and floral scents. It contains notes of melon and pear with hyacinth, jasmine, rose and lily. At the base, there's a wake of musk and sandalwood. That means that this scent is as manly as it is intoxicating.

## 3. COMME DES GARCONS

![COMME DES GARCONS](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fi/al/hnpjwfco56322ae80187a964648537_600x515.jpg)

$75 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Yes, this one is a little pricy. Of course, if you want to get your boyfriend an expensive gift for the holidays, he'll love this one. It contains rich top notes and spicy undertones. Want more specifics? It has notes of Madagascan Pepper, Bergamot, Somalian Incense, Nutmeg, Cristalon, Cashmeran, Gaïacwood, Cedarwood, Carvi Graines, Javanol, Sandalwood, Vetiver, and Agarwood.

## 4. The Curve

![The Curve](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k7/g7/ukyr7n4v56322ae614f98357247654_600x600.jpg)

$45 at [kohls.com](http://www.kohls.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Not only is this scent impressive, but the bottle it comes in is pretty impressive as well. If your man likes to fill his shelves with nicknacks, then he should enjoy the bottle. Meanwhile, he'll enjoy the scent, which contains notes of lavender, clove, bergamot, vetiver, musk, lime, citrus, sandalwood and cedar.

## 5. Cool Water Echo

![Cool Water Echo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ve/gk/b0o2d4k956322ae69bf16011005785_600x591.jpg)

$24 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

If your man needs something to wear to work, this fragrance is perfect for him. It works best in the day time, because it contains notes of lavender, jasmine, oakmoss, musk and sandalwood. If that combination sounds sweet to you now, wait until you actually get a whiff of it. It's intoxicating.

## 6. HERM S Terre D'Herm S

![HERM S Terre D'Herm S](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gf/k3/gnhivz7y56322ae690be4979692849_250x250.jpg)

$11 at [fragrancenet.com](http://www.fragrancenet.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is another daytime fragrance. However, this one is one of the best, which is why it's super expensive. If you want to buy an affordable bottle, you should stick to getting a small one. Why would you want it? Well, it contains notes of grapefruit, bay rose, pepper, geranium, Atlas cedar, benzoin, patchouli, and vetiver.

## 7. Ralph Lauren Polo Blue

![Ralph Lauren Polo Blue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kl/in/xuoxiyu856322ae7711e8440757400_355x272.jpg)

$36 at [macys.com](http://www1.macys.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

You already knew that Ralph Lauren made amazing clothes, but did you know that they make fabulous fragrances as well? This is one of their best ones, because it contains top notes of melon, mandarin orange and cucumber. It contains middle notes of basil, sage and geranium. It contains base notes of musk, woodsy notes and suede.

Men get turned on by looking at women, but we get turned on by the scent of men. That's why you'll really be doing yourself a favor if you buy your man one of these delicious scents. What's your favorite scent to smell on a man?
