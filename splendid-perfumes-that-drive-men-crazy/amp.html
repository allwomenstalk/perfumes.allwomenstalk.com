7 Splendid Perfumes That Drive Men Crazy ...

La Perouse by Gilly Hicks • Weekend for Women by Burberry • Gucci by Gucci • Boudoir by Vivienne Westwood • Hypnotic Poison by Dior • More ...

By Heather

There are tons of different perfumes that drive men crazy, but do you know them all? I've surveyed a ton of different men and I've found all of the different perfumes that drive men crazy and will make your man addicted to your scent! So, whether you are a Gucci girl or a Burberry chick, check out all of my top perfumes that will really make your man go completely nuts for you!

1. La Perouse by Gilly Hicks

Source

Most people don't know about this amazing perfume, nor do they think that this is one of the top perfumes that drive men crazy but it is! It's a beautiful scent of fresh flowers, soft woods and a bit of lilac and soft musk. It's an addicting yet not overwhelming or overpowering scent. Guys love it, girls, so give it a try for your man and yourself!

2. Weekend for Women by Burberry

Source

If you are looking for a scent that is a little more on the relaxed side and a little on the sweet and zesty side, this is it! It's got some grassy notes, peach flower, wild rose and also some blue hyacinth, as well as iris and nectarine. It's a scent that unfolds on your skin and it's truthfully an amazing scent to spray on!

3. Gucci by Gucci

Source

Who doesn't love an elegant perfume that is ideal for date nights or even those hot and steamy nights at home? This scent is all about depth and complexity. It's got a bit of musk to it, along with some smoky leather, orange blossom and white flower. It's truthfully one of my favorite scents! Have you ever tried any Gucci perfumes? Let us know about them!

4. Boudoir by Vivienne Westwood

Source

Ah, Vivienne Westwood really knows what she is doing when it comes to perfumes! This scent is extremely feminine and totally floral. It's got all sorts of floral notes, from mandarin all the way to red English roses and finally finishing off with some tobacco scents!

5. Hypnotic Poison by Dior

Source

If you are looking for a perfume that is amazingly mysterious, that is absolutely beautiful as it unfolds and that offers a luscious contrast, this is it! It's got some bitter almond notes, some jasmine, cinnamon and some vanilla scents to it. It's honestly a bewitching scent that will have him hypnotized!

6. Black Orchid by Tom Ford

Source

If you are looking for a sensual, timeless smell, this is it! It's got some orchid notes, patchouli, spicy floral accord, mandarin and of course some citrus in it! It's a scent that unfolds and a scent that so many men have fallen in love with! Give it a try and let us know how you like it!

7. Miss Dior Cherie

Source

Finally, this is the last scent that we have that will drive your man completely insane! It's pure, it's precious and it's totally innocent – even if you aren't! It's got some Egyptian jasmine notes, some patchouli notes and finally some mandarin essence. I love this smell because it is so, so innocent and so pure -- have you ever tried it?

So girls, now that you know all of the different scents that will drive men crazy, do you have any more? What do you wear to drive your man insane? Give it up!

Frequently Asked Questions

What are perfumes that men like?
Men are often attracted to scents that are fresh, floral, sweet, or have a hint of spice. Each man might have a different preference.
Can perfume affect attraction?
Yes, perfume can play a significant role in attraction. Certain scents can evoke memories, emotions, and can be quite alluring to others.
Are there specific ingredients in perfumes that men love?
Many men enjoy perfumes with vanilla, jasmine, lavender, sandalwood, and musk. These ingredients are known for their appealing and captivating scents.
Do men prefer strong or light perfumes?
This depends on personal taste, but many men lean towards perfumes that are not overpowering but have a long-lasting, subtle fragrance.
Is it important to consider the occasion when choosing a perfume to attract men?
Yes, the occasion can influence the choice of perfume. Lighter scents are great for daytime wear, while more intense fragrances might be better for evening events.
How can I test if a perfume will be appealing to men?
You can observe reactions when wearing the perfume or directly ask for opinions from friends or a partner. Sampling a perfume on your skin is the best way to test its true scent.
Does personal body chemistry affect how a perfume smells?
Absolutely. Each person's body chemistry can alter the way a perfume smells, so a fragrance might smell different on you compared to someone else.
Can wearing a certain perfume boost my confidence?
Yes, wearing a perfume you love can boost your confidence and make you feel more attractive, which can, in turn, be appealing to others.
Are there any perfumes that are universally attractive to men?
No perfume is universally attractive to all men as scent preferences are subjective. However, popular choices often become well-liked due to their appealing blend of notes.
How long does a perfume's scent last?
Perfumes can last anywhere from a few hours to all day, depending on the formulation and concentration of the fragrance oils used.
Can I wear men's perfume to attract men?
Yes, many men find women who wear traditionally 'masculine' scents intriguing. It's all about what you feel comfortable with and what suits your personal style.
