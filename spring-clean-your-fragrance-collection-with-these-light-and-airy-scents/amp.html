By Neecey

When the dark nights and dismal days of winter have been elbowed out by spring, we make changes to many of our routines. Out come the lighter clothes, we search for new beachwear, and we’re glad to slip our bare feet into sandals instead of heavy boots. So doesn’t it make sense to lighten and freshen up your fragrance too? Here’s my suggestions for perfect spring perfumes:

1. Tom Ford Neroli Portofino Acqua

You’ll be hard pushed to find any perfume that is more suited to warm days than this exquisite fragrance from Tom Ford. Bursting with bundles of citrus notes, it’s so fresh you’ll want to wear it in winter too!

2. Maison Francis Kurkdjian Vita Eau De Toilette

Another scent that gives you a fresh feel with lots of citrus, but this particular fragrance is somewhat softened with Brazilian tonka bean and a dash of vanilla.

3. Byredo Gypsy Water

Byredo Gypsy Water, quite honestly, is a winner all year round, but its vanilla and sandalwood notes are perfect for any hot day.

4. Lemon Mary Greenwell Parfum Spray

Frequently Asked Questions

What are light and airy scents?
They are perfumes with fresh, delicate notes that feel uplifting and perfect for warmer weather.
Why choose light scents for spring?
Spring's warmer temperatures pair well with refreshing, subtle fragrances that don’t feel heavy.
What ingredients are common in airy perfumes?
Common notes include citrus, cotton, white florals, and soft musk.
Can I wear light scents year-round?
Absolutely! They’re versatile but especially refreshing in spring and summer.
How do I apply light perfumes for best effect?
Apply to pulse points like wrists and behind ears for a gentle, lasting scent.
Are light scents suitable for work environments?
Yes, their subtlety makes them ideal for professional settings.
How long do light perfumes typically last?
They tend to last 3-5 hours but can be refreshed easily throughout the day.
Can layering scents make light perfumes last longer?
Yes, layering with matching body lotions can enhance longevity.
What are some popular light perfume brands for spring?
Brands like Jo Malone, Clinique, and Marc Jacobs offer great light options.
How do I choose the right light scent for me?
Test scents on your skin and pick one that makes you feel fresh and confident.
