# The Best Perfumes to Wear after a Major Sweat Session ...

You don't want to spray on a perfume that's way too heavy after exercising, because it can create a strange odor when it mixes with your sweat. That's why you shouldn't wear your regular perfume after the gym. You should buy a separate bottle that'll work well for the occasion. If you're not sure where to look, here are a few of the best perfumes to wear after exercising:

## 1. Revlon Lasting

![Revlon Lasting](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rf/g4/fyp1v9zy563b6050cab6d235253892_600x471.jpg)

[amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This perfume has a blend of floral and fruity scents. It has notes of lotus and pear, which smell intoxicating. That's why it's perfect to wear during any time of the year. However, it works best during the daytime--but since most people tend to work out during the early hours of the day, that shouldn't be a problem for you.

## 2. Dolce & Gabbana Light Blue

![Dolce & Gabbana Light Blue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bn/gb/jdzzsxog563b60530ff4b267012035_600x582.jpg)

[amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is a fresh, citrusy scent. That's why you'll want to apply it right after exercising. It'll make you smell like you've just stepped out of the shower instead of like you've just stepped off of the treadmill. It contains notes of apple, cedar, lemon, and bellflower.

## 3. Giorgio Beverly Hills Sunshine

![Giorgio Beverly Hills Sunshine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v3/fv/nxotkwis563b6051942dd931156452_522x522.jpg)

[amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This perfume has a white floral scent. Its bottle is just as sexy as its smell, which contains notes of lily, orange, papaya, and lemon tree. It works best in the daytime, so make sure you bring it with you during your morning run. Then you'll feel gorgeous for the rest of the day.

## 4. Cartier Eau De Cartier

![Cartier Eau De Cartier](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/am/s0/rk7vmmto563b6051550d7414045101_600x600.jpg)

[amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This has a citrus scent that's a little bit spicy. It's strong enough to cover up the smell of your sweat, but it's not so strong that it'll hurt your nose. It strikes the perfect balance. What's inside of it? Well, it contains notes of violet, yuzu, and coriander.

## 5. Fendi Palazzo Fendi Eau De Toilette

![Fendi Palazzo Fendi Eau De Toilette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sy/tg/wvcq2977563b605183f59727915500_500x464.jpg)

[amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is another perfume with a strong citrus scent. It works just as well during the night as it does during the day, which means you can use it if you're one of the rare people who head to the gym right before bed. It contains delicious notes of lemon, orange, and patchouli.

## 6. Victoria's Secret Crush

_ victoriassecret.com_

You can trust Victoria's Secret when it comes to bras, underwear, and yoga pants. Of course, you can trust them when it comes to perfumes, as well. They create topnotch scents, like this one, which contains notes of pink peppercorn, treasure peony and ashok flower for a fresh and energetic fragrance.

## 7. Eau D'Hadrien

![Eau D'Hadrien](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v0/eg/yl08y978563b605373609946906137_600x554.jpg)

[amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Yes, this one is pretty expensive. However, it's raved about by all of the women who wear it, which is why it's worth its high price. It has a citrus scent with notes of lemon, grapefruit, cypress, and citron. It's one of the best scents you can wear during the day, even if you decide to skip the gym completely.

You don't want to smell disgusting after the gym, which is why you have to be careful when you pick your perfume. Make sure you buy a good one and keep it in your gym bag, so that no one has a clue you've just finished an intense workout. Which scent do you usually spritz on after the gym?
