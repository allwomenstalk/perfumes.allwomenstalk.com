# The 21 Perfumes Youll Want to Wear around the Holidays ...

With so many new perfumes on the market this holiday season, it's daunting to decide which will be your new signature scent. But not to worry! I'm compiled a list of all-time classics and brand-new favorites from our most-loved designers, and there's bound to be a perfect perfume to take you from the turkey at Thanksgiving to your New Year's Eve kiss and beyond.

## 1. My Burberry Festive

![My Burberry Festive](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f0/xg/t0v5qxc35651fa841c693637175025_600x583.jpg)

Price: $125.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Inspired by a London garden after the rain, this brand-new scent is light and floral enough to wear into spring.

## 2. Bvlgari Omnia Paraiba

![Bvlgari Omnia Paraiba](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r3/z9/uwwdmvm25651fa8300575566542730_600x582.jpg)

Price: $82.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This bold,energetic perfume is named for, and inspired by, the exotic Paraiba tourmaline.

## 3. Michael Kors Coral

![Michael Kors Coral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l3/ag/u8f9a2zh5651fa8432c06548907617_600x584.jpg)

## 4. Commodity Magnolia
