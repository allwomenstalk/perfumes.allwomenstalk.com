# The Sweetest Scents You Can Wear to School ...

For school you don’t want a heavy cloying perfume that is a pervasive miasma in a crowded lecture hall. You want a delicious scent that your schoolmates will be thankful is filling up the hallways! Your perfume should say “hey this is me” as well as smell fabulous. Like these:

## 1. Tommy Girl – Tommy Hilfiger

![Tommy Girl – Tommy Hilfiger](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ah/rg/l6zb8frh561699f900f9b444863201_600x600.jpg)

With notes of heather and sandalwood that are warm and refreshing at the same time, it’s great for some daytime sophistication.

Buy at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Princess – Vera Wang

Wear this if you are independent and playful. It has a flirty and adventurous vibe with overtones of the sweetest cupcake in the bakery.

![Princess – Vera Wang](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/it/rj/pzoby5c5561699b7061ad976345199_400x302.jpg)

Buy at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Infinite Pleasure – Just Girl – Estelle Vendome

A great in-between scent, fruity enough for a fun day at school but still with a few musky undertones that show you are more of an adult than a child.

![Infinite Pleasure – Just Girl – Estelle Vendome](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/it/qj/dnqmxs8o561699b792c60490229125_522x298.jpg)

Buy at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. Live Colorfully – Kate Spade

Citrus and florals open this sweet scent, but a lingering hint of musk stays with you long after the top notes have dissipated.

![Live Colorfully – Kate Spade](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/av/gi/x85o3a70561699b82165a414094573_600x711.jpg)

Buy at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Unpredictable Girl – Glenn Perri

This scent is really light hearted, a great choice for a carefree teenager. It’s very sweet with notes of vanilla, jasmine, cedar wood and frangipani and a dash of neroli essential oils.

![Unpredictable Girl – Glenn Perri](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lr/ql/lfdsixxy561699b8413ad335678382_522x336.jpg)

Buy at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Citrine Blossom – Coach

A spritz of this zesty scent will get your mornings off to an invigorating start.

![Citrine Blossom – Coach](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j4/j9/qjdc62ht561699ba0d6d8501028916_600x559.jpg)

Buy at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 7. Avon – Bond Girl Forever

Indulge in a little bit of sultriness at school without taking it too far. Feels light and appropriate for school due to its overriding fruity citrus tone.

![Avon – Bond Girl Forever](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yf/yf/fc3e0zcf561699b8a6bce671911489_522x520.jpg)

## 8. Fantasy – Britney Spears

Feminine and invigorating, Fantasy is a fun and lively fragrance for daytime.

![Fantasy – Britney Spears](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qu/tq/q3efdyr8561699bd1e71d839452387_600x784.jpg)

## 14. Peony and Blush Suede Cologne - Jo Malone

Peonies, red apple, jasmine, and rose make this perfume the perfect daytime go-to.

![Peony and Blush Suede Cologne - Jo Malone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jk/n5/hqcuv8kz561699bdd746e934782767_600x871.jpg)
