These 23 Floral Perfumes Will Make Him Swoon on Valentine's Day ...

Tocca Florence • Viktor &amp; Rolf Flowerbomb • Versace Crystal Noir • Yves Saint Laurent Parisienne • Gucci Bamboo • More ...

By Jennifer

Want to make his head swim this Valentine's Day? Why not try a new floral perfume, a light, feminine scent that's sure give him spring fever in the middle of February. Here are a few of my fave florals to give you a some fresh, flirty ideas.

1. Tocca Florence

Price: $72 at sephora.com
An elegant gardenia with blonde wood blend for a rush of feminine and flirty vibes.

2. Viktor & Rolf Flowerbomb

Price: $80.00 at sephora.com
As the name implies, this is the ultimate floral scent.

3. Versace Crystal Noir

Price: $73 at sephora.com
This perfume, described as floral-oriental, is the perfect hybrid of seductive and sweet with gardenia, tuberose, and amber.

4. Yves Saint Laurent Parisienne

Price: $39 at sephora.com
Feminine and fruity with blackberry top notes and damask rose heart.

5. Gucci Bamboo

Price: $92.00 at sephora.com
This perfume is surprisingly soft and feminine, but strong... a little goes a long way.

6. Marc Jacobs Daisy

Price: $78.00 at sephora.com
This is my favorite floral scent, so light and flirty and fun!

7. Versace Bright Crystal

Price: $73.00 at sephora.com
This light, bright scent has peony and magnolia tones.

8. Stella McCartney Pop

Price: $72 at sephora.com
The irresistible floral and woody notes of this fragrance will instantly put a smile on your date’s face.

9. Tornade Blonde Christian Louboutin

Price: $275 at sephora.com
Envelop his senses in red rose, violet, and cassis. The next best thing to a bed of roses.

10. Ralph Lauren Midnight Romance

Price: $76.00 at sephora.com
This is my favorite Ralph Lauren scent, very sweet and romantic.

11. My Burberry Eau De Toilette

Price: $78.00 at sephora.com
In a word, this perfume is cheery. Truly, that's the best way to describe it.

12. Jo Malone Nasturtium & Clover Cologne

Price: $70 at jomalone.com
Bright, warm, and instantly addictive-just like you!

13. Hanae Mori Butterfly Eau De Parfum

Price: $75.00 at sephora.com
This perfume is very unique, floral and berry-scented together.

14. Walk on Air Kate Spade New York

Price: $75 at sephora.com
Make him feel like he’s walking on air with this sensual perfume with layers of lilies and magnolia.

15. Tory Burch Jolie Fleur Verte

Price: $95 at sephora.com
This perfume captures the fresh and dewy fragrance of lilies and jasmine.

16. Viva La Juicy Rosé Juicy Couture

Price: $76 at sephora.com
The perfect fragrance for those who like their perfumes to be flirty and floral with a dash of fruity notes.

17. FlowerbyKenzo

Price: $59.00 at sephora.com
See that poppy on the bottle? That's your first hint of the major tons of this perfume, with vanilla, rose, and violet for good measure.

18. Flora by Gucci Eau De Parfum

Price: $70.00 at sephora.com
This is an iconic, classic floral scent, very sweet but subtle.

19. LAVANILA Vanilla Lavender Fragrance

Price: $58 at sephora.com
Vanilla and lavender is one of the best fragrance combinations! It’s warm, romantic, and modern.

20. Modern Muse Estee Lauder

Price: $65 at sephora.com
An alluring floral perfume mixed with sensual woods accord.

21. Endless Euphoria Calvin Klein

Price: $74 at sephora.com
Cherry blossoms, bamboo, and violet make this a provocative scent that was made for lovers.

22. Korres Velvet Orris Violet White Pepper Eau De Toilette

Price: $38.50 at sephora.com
Don’t let the minimalist design fool you! This fragrance is sharp, spicy, and truly euphoric.

23. Givenchy Dahlia Noir

Price: $75 at sephora.com
This perfume features distinctive notes of seductive rose, amber, pink pepper, and more.
