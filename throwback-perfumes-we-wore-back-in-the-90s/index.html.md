# 17 Throwback Perfumes We Wore Back in the '90s ...

Ah, the 1990s – the decade of magic. Those of us who grew up in the '90s look back on it with such misty nostalgia, fondly remembering the music, the movies, and the makeup. Ask us and we'll happily tell you how awesome the '90s were and how almost everything was better. That's … not necessarily true, of course, but that's how we remember it. So in the spirit of nostalgia, let's take a look at some of the standout perfumes from the '90s, shall we? At the end, check off how many of them you wore!

## 1. GAP Dream & Heaven

This was the perfume you wore when you were working that preppy vibe. It somehow complimented your Delia's tops and your Abercrombie & Fitch shorts.

## 2. Elizabeth Arden Sunflowers

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/io/nu/g0j0buq5571e8686445af079805733_600x600.jpg)

[@justdntgiveup](https://www.instagram.com/p/BAqntP7EJww/?tagged=elizabethardensunflowers)

Sunflowers was the go-to perfume for girls who wanted to give off a bright, nature-loving, sunshine kind of vibe. Unfortunately, you usually just ended up smelling like a vat full of sunflowers.

## 3. The Body Shop White Musk

Oh, I miss this, I admit it. White musk scents are always so soft and pretty.

## 4. Tommy Girl

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b2/tp/ivrrku9q571e8687a80d4624452872_600x749.jpg)

[@jananna301](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

Tommy Girl was kind of like Dream & Heaven, in that – in my high school, at least – the popular, preppy girls were always mad for it.

## 5. Vanilla Fields

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ns/ga/muyf0xjq571e8687f2f6f314092021_600x600.jpg)

[@_cheriana_](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

I admit, I hated this perfume. I still hate it. To be fair, however, it's only because a girl I despised wore it all the time. You hear that, Cassie? I still hate you.

## 6. Estee Lauder Pleasures

This is what you wore when you wanted to feel oh so grown up and sophisticated. It always worked.

## 7. Bath & Body Works Country Apple

![Bath & Body Works Country Apple](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p8/g5/m83igfoo571e867ebb6cd820822739_580x580.jpg)

Oh, god, Country Apple smelled so delicious. I mean, it kind of made a better air freshener than a perfume, but still.

## 8. Tommy Hilfiger Freedom

Freedom always made you think about the end of school. It managed to smell like late spring and early summer.

## 9. Gucci Rush

Anything Gucci was reserved for special occasions – prom, Homecoming, graduation. You didn't waste this stuff.

## 10. Clinique Happy

Tell me this didn't smell like youth in a bottle.

## 11. The SPICE GIRLS IMPULSE BODY SPRAY

![The SPICE GIRLS IMPULSE BODY SPRAY](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jt/l3/l145hzyy571e86c70d6de601906847_500x500.jpg)

Everyone was mad for this, but I thought it smelled terrible. Still, Spice Girls!

## 12. Coty EXCLAMATION

My BFF wore this. I can never smell it without thinking of her.

## 13. LOVE'S BABY SOFT

Love's Baby Soft was THE perfume of the '90s, practically. It was also like My First Perfume because it was the only one your parents would let you wear at first. Remember when Love's came out with a whole line of these?

## 14. CK One

I still have a bottle of this hanging around somewhere. It's so old it probably doesn't even smell like One anymore, though.

## 15. LANCOME POEME

True facts, I bought this for the name. Back when I still wore perfume, that is.

## 16. DAVIDOFF COOL WATER for WOMEN

This is the one perfume that I still adore – it's so bright and fresh!

## 17. CANDIE'S for HER

![CANDIE'S for HER](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g2/sq/grjkglay571e8685d6892058868148_600x545.jpg)

Candie's was the shit in the '90s and early 2000s. Not only were the perfumes and colognes delicious, but the shoes, man, the shoes!

So, did you wear any of these? Miss any of them?
