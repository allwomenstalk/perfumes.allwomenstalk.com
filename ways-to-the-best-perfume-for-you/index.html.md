# 7 Ways to the Best Perfume for You ...

Best Perfumes are different for every woman. This is because every woman has a different chemistry and her skin will mix differently with each perfume she tries. So if you want to know ways to the best perfume for you, you need to decide which category you fit into. There are different categories of perfume, and I've made a list with different ways to the best perfume for you. If you have been wanting to try wearing perfume, but aren't sure which scent is right for you, keep reading! I've got some expert tips for you to follow to finding the best perfume for you!

## 1. Floral

If you are a girly-girl, chances are your best perfume will be a floral fragrance. Floral perfumes have flowery notes and often have flowery names. They smell sweet and usually are very strong. Just use a few drops!

## 2. Fruity

My best perfume is fruity perfumes! I love anything with a hint of fruity notes. Citrus and orchard hints along with jasmine makes a great smelling perfume! Most fruity scents are lighter than floral ones, so you can wear a little more of a [fruity perfume](https://perfumes.allwomenstalk.com/fruity-fabulous-perfumes-to-try/) to get a stronger scent going.

## 3. Musk

Musk perfumes tend to have stronger, deeper and more passionate hints to them. Vanilla is usually a main component in these types of perfume. If you are a passionate, romantic person, try a musk perfume. My guess is that it will be your best perfume!

## 4. Fresh

Perfumes with a fresh scent to them are usually for someone who doesn't like to wear a strong scent or is new to perfume. Think of a clean smell, like soap or powder and if that appeals to you, your best perfume would be something that smelled light and fresh. Hey, everybody likes to smell clean and sweet, right?

## 5. Bottle Styles

A word about perfume bottle styles. If you have found your best perfume, then you should be happy with the style of bottle it has too! Some perfume bottles have a spray pump, others are a roll-on style. Some are meant to be dabbed on, then others come in a solid form that you rub onto your skin. Find the formula that works for you and stick with it!

## 6. Perfume Type

Don't shy away from a perfume just because you think it's too strong for you. If you have found your best perfume, check to see if it comes in other forms. A perfume is very heavy and concentrated, so if you like the scent, yet want something lighter, look for it in a body spray. An even lighter way to wear perfume is through scented body washes and lotions!

## 7. Application

Now that you have found your best perfume, you'll want to know where to apply it. I like to apply perfume right behind my ears, on my wrists and behind my knees. These are some major pulse points and will help emit the scent all day long!

Now that you have these tips on ways to the best perfume for you, you'll be able to smell great with the rest of them! Don't hesitate to get out there and find your signature scent! Did you learn anything about finding the Best Perfume for you from my article?
